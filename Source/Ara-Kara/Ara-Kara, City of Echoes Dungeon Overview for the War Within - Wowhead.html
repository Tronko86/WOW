Ara-Kara, City of Echoes Dungeon Overview for the War Within

Di Paryah Aggiornato: 12/08/2025
[Live]
Paryah
[Follow]:
This guide to Ara-Kara, Città degli Echi includes the dungeon location and how to get there, bosses and boss abilities, and all relevant loot that drops in the dungeon, as well as all achievements that can be earned.

For information about Mythic+ dungeons, including which dungeons are active in this seasonal rotation and strategy guides for each, check out our Mythic+ Season Guide.

The War Within Season 3 Mythic+ GuideThe War Within Season 3 Mythic+ Guide


Ara-Kara, City of Echoes is a 3 boss dungeon located in Azj-Kahet. An old city beneath the City of Threads, Ara-Kara is the site of Ansurek's excavation to tap into the Old Gods.
Just beneath the City of Threads lies Ara-Kara, City of Echoes, the deserted ruins of the City's previous layer. There, Ansurek's forces seek to harvest enough deadly materials to put the entirety of Nerubian civilization through ascension if necessary - whether they are willing or not.

Ara-Kara Mythic+ Guide Cheat Sheet for the War Within

How to get to Ara-Kara in the War Within

The entrance to Ara-Kara is located at /way 52.1 45.7. This is in the City of Threads, in the southwest of Azj-Kahet. Coordinates for the Azj-Kahet map are /way 49.4 80.8.

Dungeon Map for Ara-Kara in the War Within

Ara-Kara has 2 floors. You will enter the dungeon and descend into the Echoing Overlook where you will encounter Avanoxx. At the far end of the Overlook, after defeating Anub'zekt, you will be able to descend into the Undercache where the final boss, Ki'katal the Harvester, awaits.

Summoning Avanoxx

In order to summon the first boss of this Dungeon, players will need to defeat the three shrilling voice mini-bosses: Ixin, Nakt, and Atik. These are located on the outside border of the Avanoxx boss arena. We have provided pins for each mini-boss on the dungeon map above.


Defeat the three Shrilling Voices to summon Avanoxx!

Interactive Dungeon Buff

Throughout this dungeon, there are numerous Attendente Discordante that Khaz Algar Tailors of at least Skill 25 can interact with. This gives the Tailor an on-use known as Filo di Seta that can be used on mobs to stun them for 10 seconds. This can be used on the Shrilling Voice mini-bosses!


Discordant Attendants give Tailors a 10 second Filo di Seta Stun!

Dungeon Waystones for Ara-Kara, City of Echoes

There are three Waystones that serve as dungeon checkpoints for Ara-Kara, City of Echoes. The first Waystone unlocks after defeating Avanoxx with each subsequent Waystone unlocking after defeating later bosses. Keep in mind that once a new Waystone unlocks, the previous ones can no longer be accessed.

 
The first Waystone (left) unlocks after defeating Avanoxx with the next Waystone (right) unlocking after defeating Anub'zekt



The final Waystone unlocks after defeating Ki'katal the Harvester

Ara-Kara Bosses in the War Within

Deserted when Ara-Kara fell to ruin, the creature known as Avanoxx started as nothing more than a humble spider. Years of isolation in an area ripe for feeding have left her free to grow, overseeing her children, her followers, and her territory with unmatched size and ferocity.

Overview

Avanoxx consumes with Morso Vorace and signals to nearby Strisciatore Affamato with Stridio d'Allerta that grow in Fame over time. Avanoxx unleashes Ragnatela Massacrante and causes additional undercrawler eggs to fall from above. Ragnatela Vile serves as the perfect trap for her prey.

In Mythic difficulty, Avanoxx becomes Insaziabile

Tank:
  • Morso Vorace inflicts heavy damage to its target and increases damage taken for a period of time.
  • Standing in Ragnatela Vile causes Ragnatela Avvolgente.
  • Fame causes Starved Crawlers to fixate on other players.
Damage Dealers:
  • Standing in Ragnatela Vile causes Ragnatela Avvolgente.
  • Starved Crawlers grow in Fame over time.
Healer:
  • Standing in Ragnatela Vile causes Ragnatela Avvolgente.
  • Stridio d'Allerta and Ragnatela Massacrante inflict substantial party-wide damage.
  • Morso Vorace inflicts heavy damage to its target and increases damage taken for a period of time.

Abilities

  • Insaziabile: Avanoxx banchetta con gli Strisciatori Affamati che la raggiungono, aumentando i danni inflitti del 30% per 1 min. Si accumula.
  • Stridio d'Allerta: Avanoxx emette uno stridio assordante, richiamando gli Strisciatori Affamati dalle loro uova e infliggendo 848557 danni fisici ogni 0.5 s a tutti i personaggi per 3 s.
    • Strisciatore Affamato
      Fame: Lo Strisciatore Affamato prende di mira un personaggio casuale e aumenta i danni inflitti del 10% ogni 3 s. Si accumula.
  • Ragnatela Massacrante: Avanoxx lancia ragnatele tossiche in tutte le direzioni, infliggendo 909168 danni da natura ogni 1 s a tutti i personaggi per 5 s.
    Inoltre, lascia dietro di sé Ragnatela Vile sulle posizioni dei bersagli, infliggendo 4848895 danni da natura a tutti i personaggi entro 2.5 m da ogni punto d'impatto.
    • Ragnatela Vile: Una Ragnatela Vile intrappola i personaggi all'interno, riducendone la velocità di movimento del 50% e infliggendo 969779 danni da natura ogni 1 s. Si accumula.
      Al raggiungimento di 5 accumuli, la ragnatela si sviluppa in una Ragnatela Avvolgente.
      •   Ragnatela Avvolgente: La Ragnatela Avvolgente circonda completamente il personaggio, inabilitandolo per 10 s.
  •   Morso Vorace: Avanoxx balza sul bersaglio primario e scatena 3 morsi consecutivi, ciascuno dei quali infligge 6061118 danni fisici. Il morso finale aumenta ulteriormente i danni subiti dal bersaglio del 50% per 10 s.
While Anub'zekt has not yet earned his own ascension, he has been granted the honor of overseeing the Ara-Kara harvesting operation. Fiercely tactical and immensely strong, Anub'zekt will not hesitate to step in should his forces prove insufficient in their defense of Ara-Kara.

Overview

Anub'zekt fights fiercely alongside his brood, attacking with brutal Impalamento attacks while Sciame Incessante roam the battlefield. When he's decide it's time to end things, he calls upon his entire brood, dragging enemies into the Occhio dello Sciame where they can't escape.

Tanks:
  • Keep Anub'zekt facing away from your allies to keep them safe from Impalamento.
  • During Occhio dello Sciame, it will be even harder to keep allies safe from Impalamento.
Damage Dealers:
  • Watch out for players affected by Infestazione and the Sciame Incessante that spawns from them.
  • The farther you are from Anub'zekt during their Carica del Sottosuolo, the easier it is to dodge.
Healers:
  • Watch out for players affected by Infestazione and the Sciame Incessante that spawns from them.
  • During Occhio dello Sciame, it will be even harder to keep allies safe from Infestazione and Sciame Incessante.

Abilities

  •   Impalamento: Anub'zekt lancia un cono di spuntoni attraverso il terreno verso il suo bersaglio attuale, infliggendo 12122236 danni fisici ai personaggi colpiti e respingendoli indietro.
  • Carica del Sottosuolo: Anub'zekt si rintana sotto un personaggio casuale, infliggendo 4848895 danni fisici e respingendo indietro i personaggi sulla traiettoria.
    Alla fine della traiettoria, erompe dalla terra, infliggendo 6061118 danni fisici e respingendo indietro i personaggi entro 13 m.
  •   Infestazione: Anub'zekt richiama uno sciame che banchetta con un personaggio casuale, infliggendo 545501 danni da natura ogni 0.5 s per 5 s.
    Se dissolto, lo sciame si trasforma in uno Sciame Incessante e va in cerca di altre prede.
    •   Sciame Incessante: Lo Sciame Incessante si aggira nell'arena, cercando personaggi da divorare. Quando entra in contatto con un personaggio, gli procura ferite putrescenti, infliggendo 969779 danni da natura ogni 0.5 s e riducendone la velocità di movimento del 3% per 2 s. Si accumula.
  •   Occhio dello Sciame: Quando raggiunge 100 energia, Anub'zekt richiama un massiccio sciame che circonda l'arena. I personaggi all'interno dello sciame d'insetti ottengono rapidamente accumuli di Sciame Incessante.
  • Mago delle Tele Macchiato di Sangue: Occasionally, a Bloodstained Webmage will spawn in the arena to assist Anub'zekt.
    • Catene di Seta: Il Mago delle Tele Macchiato di Sangue avvolge il bersaglio in una ragnatela stretta e porosa, immobilizzandolo e infliggendo 1454668 danni da natura ogni 1 s per 15 s.
As one of Ansurek's ascended, Ki'katal la Mietitrice has been charged with overseeing the harvest of Black Blood from the very feet of the Queen's own citadel. Harsh and unrelenting, Ki'katal will stop at nothing to see the harvest continue, utilitzing every enhanced ability at her disposal.

Overview

A stream of Bloodworkers ferry Sangue Nero for Ki'katal the Harvester. When struck, they drop their slime cargo and flee. Upon reaching 100 energy, Ki'katal the Harvester manifests a Singolarità Cosmica, pulling all players in towards the detonation.

Tanks:
  • Stepping within Black Blood causes Sangue Ghermitore to root players in place and prevent them from moving.
  • Singolarità Cosmica pulls all players in and inflicts high damage.
Damage Dealers:
  • Stepping within Black Blood causes Sangue Ghermitore to root players in place and prevent them from moving.
  • Singolarità Cosmica pulls all players in and inflicts high damage.
Healers:
  • Stepping within Black Blood causes Sangue Ghermitore to root players in place and prevent them from moving.
  • Singolarità Cosmica pulls all players in and inflicts high damage.

Abilities

  • Bloodworkers: Bloodworkers carry Black Blood. Any damage taken causes the drone to drop it and flee.
    • Sangue Ghermitore: Entrare in una pozza di Sangue Nero lo fa condensare, immobilizzando il bersaglio sul posto per 1 min e infliggendo 484889 danni da ombra ogni 1 s. Distruggere il Sangue Nero permette di liberare il personaggio.
      On Mythic difficulty, Black Blood is absorbed after a single player steps in it.
  •   Singolarità Cosmica: Ki'katal la Mietitrice trascina a sé tutti i personaggi e inizia a creare una distorsione spaziale. Al termine, tutti i personaggi entro 10 m subiscono 7273342 danni da ombra e vengono respinti indietro.
    • Scomparsa: Quando un personaggio viene colpito da Singolarità Cosmica, la sua essenza si dissolve parzialmente, riducendone i danni inflitti del 75% e aumentandone i danni subiti del 75% per 20 s.
  •   Veleni Coltivati: Ki'katal la Mietitrice inietta un veleno crescente in 3 personaggi, infliggendo 1091001 danni da natura ogni 1 s per 8 s. Se dissolto, il veleno erutta in ondate e infligge 3636671 danni da natura e 969779 danni da natura aggiuntivi ogni 0.5 s per 5 s ai personaggi colpiti.
  •   Raffica di Veleno: Raffica di Veleno
  • Ragnatele Eruttive: Ki'katal la Mietitrice provoca diverse eruzioni di ragnatele, infliggendo 8000676 danni da natura e stordendo i personaggi colpiti per 3 s.

Achievements for Ara-Kara in the War Within

There are 4 Achievements that can be earned in Ara-Kara.
  • Ara-Kara, Città degli Echi
    Defeat Ki'katal the Harvester in Ara-Kara, City of Echoes.
  • Eroica: Ara-Kara, Città degli Echi
    Defeat Ki'katal the Harvester in Ara-Kara, City of Echoes on Heroic difficulty or higher.
  • Mitica: Ara-Kara, Città degli Echi
    Defeat Ki'katal the Harvester in Ara-Kara, City of Echoes on Mythic or Mythic Keystone difficulty.
  • Eroe delle chiavi: Ara-Kara, Città degli Echi
    Complete Ara-Kara, City of Echoes at Mythic Level 10 or higher, within the time limit.
    Feat of Strength | Reward: Teleport to Ara-Kara, City of Echoes
