Priory of the Sacred Flame Dungeon Overview for The War Within

Di iMX3 Aggiornato: 12/08/2025
This guide to Prioria della Fiamma Sacra includes the dungeon location and how to get there, a boss list with abilities, and all relevant loot that drops in the dungeon, as well as all achievements that can be earned.

Priory of the Sacred Flame is featured in The War Within Mythic+ Season 3

For information about Mythic+ dungeons, including which dungeons are active in this seasonal rotation and strategy guides for each, check out our Mythic+ Season Guide.

The War Within Season 3 Mythic+ GuideThe War Within Season 3 Mythic+ Guide


Priory of the Sacred Flame is a three boss dungeon located in Hallowfall. The acolytes of the Priory have become withdrawn from the people of Mereldar, causing concern as to what secrets might lie within.
The Priory of the Sacred Flame was built for ardent worshipers to contemplate the mystery of the emperor's vision. The acolytes have become withdrawn from the people of Mereldar, looking down upon them with disdain for their weak faith. Sister Etna Blayze has called for help to investigate what secrets lay hidden in the inner sanctum of Prioress Murrpray.

    How to get to Priory of the Sacred Flame in the War Within

    Prioria della Fiamma Sacra is located at /way 42.0 50.0, in Hallowfall. This dungeon lies within the city of Mereldar, with a flight point available at /way 42.0 53.8. The dungeon entrance is at a high point in Mereldar, at a dock where dirigibles are parked.

    Dungeon Map for Priory of the Sacred Flame in the War Within

    Priory of the Sacred Flame has two parts: The outside Sanctified Grounds and the inside of the Priory itself. Players will begin on the Sanctified Grounds, where they will need to defeat Captain Dailcry. Once he is defeated, players will fight their way into the Priory where they will take on Baron Braunpyke and Prioress Murrpray.

    Priory of the Sacred Flame

    Remove the Trusted Guards!

    Before taking on Captain Dailcry, we recommended that you remove his Trusted Guards first. They are spread throughout the Sanctified Grounds: Sergeant Shaynemail, Elaena Emberlanz, and Taenar Duelmal. The Trusted Guards gain Tutti per Uno when near each other. This is a 200% damage increase per nearby member, which can become a significant problem if no guards are taken out. Here's how to draw out the Trusted Guards:
    • Sergeant Shaynemail: Make your way to the middle of the ground and take on Capitano della Guardia Suleyman. Once defeated, Shaynemail will bring the fight to you! Simply defeat her and continue moving further into the Grounds.
    • Taenar Duelmal: To the south of the main grounds is Gran Sacerdotessa Aemya, along with some other guards. Once she is defeated, Taenar will come to you. Like Shaynemail, simply defeat him and move forward.
    • Elaena Emberlanz: There is a repeating pattern here. To the north of the main grounds is Mastro Forgiatore Damian. Once he is defeated, Emberlanz will run to assist. At this point, you can defeat her.
    Each of the mini-bosses that must be defeated to summon the Trusted Guards is marked on the map above. Once 2 of the 3 Trusted Guards are defeated, Captain Dailcry will catch on to what is happening. He realizes that the players are trying to draw out his Trusted Guards before taking him on, so instead of taking on the last Trusted Guard, Captain Dailcry will come out of the Priory and face you with the last Trusted Guard beside him.
    Captain Dailcry yells: Hold! I see their gambit. They seek to ambush me without my lieutenants!

    Dungeon Waystones for Priory of the Sacred Flame

    There are three Waystones that serve as dungeon checkpoints throughout the Priory of the Sacred Flame. The first Waystone spawns after defeating Captain Dailcry with the subsequent Waystones spawning after defeating the other bosses. Keep in mind that once a new Waystone unlocks, the previous one can no longer be accessed.

     
    • Waystone #1: Unlocks after defeating Captain Dailcry
    • Waystone #2: Unlocks after defeating Baron Braunpyke
    • Waystone #3: Unlocks after defeating Prioress Murrpray

    Interactive Buff Available in Priory of the Sacred Flame

    In the center courtyard, there is a fountain with an interactable Fiamma Sacra orb in the center. Paladins and Priests can interact with this orb to give their team a 30-minute Benedizione della Fiamma Sacra buff. This buff causes all spells and abilities to inflict additional Holy damage. This buff does not persist through death.

    Priory of the Sacred Flame Bosses in the War Within

    Captain Dailcry

    Captain Dailcry commands the grounds of the Priory of the Sacred Flame. He drills the acolytes in the approved martial disciplines of the empire and ensures that their training and loyalty are of imperial quality.

    • Overview: Captain Dailcry is protected by his Trusted Guards, who gain [Strength in Numbers] while near each other. Captain Dailcry's loyal mount, Ember, ravages his enemies with [Savage Mauling] while he rallies his allies with a courageous [Battle Cry].
      • Tank:
        Captain Dailcry is protected by trusted guards who gain [Strength in Numbers].
        [Battle Cry] increases damage done by Captain Dailcry and his allies.
        [Pierce Armor] inflicts heavy bleed damage to Captain Dailcry's current target. (Mythic+, Mythic)
      • Damage Dealers:
        Captain Dailcry is protected by trusted guards who gain [Strength in Numbers].
        Ember attacks a player with [Savage Mauling] until Captain Dailcry's shield is broken.
        [Hurl Spear] inflicts heavy damage to players in the spear's path. (Mythic, Mythic+)
      • Healer:
        [Savage Mauling] inflicts heavy damage to the player caught in Ember's clutches.
        [Hurl Spear] inflicts heavy damage to players in the spear's path.
        [Pierce Armor] inflicts heavy bleed damage to Captain Dailcry's current target. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Captain Dailcry
      • Savage Mauling: Captain Dailcry commands Ember to maul a player, immobilizing them and inflicting 972986 Physical damage every 0.5 sec.
        Captain Dailcry protects Ember while she mauls her target, absorbing the next 150 damage. Breaking Dailcry's protection interrupts the effect.
      • Battle Cry: Captain Dailcry unleashes an intimidating Battle Cry inflicting 2918958 Physical damage to all players. Ignores armor.
        Captain Dailcry's Battle Cry inspires his allies, granting each of them 50 energy and increasing their damage done by 50% for 30 sec.
        This effect stacks.
      • Hurl Spear: Captain Dailcry hurls his spear towards a target, penetrating the armor of any players in its path and inflicting 7297396 Physical damage. Additionally, victims bleed for 972986 Physical damage every 0.5 sec for 8 sec.
      • Pierce Armor: Captain Dailcry slashes at his current target, inflicting 6486574 Physical damage and causing the target to bleed for 972986 Physical damge every 1 sec for 10 sec.
        This effect stacks.
    • The Trusted Guard
      • Strength in Numbers: Captain Dailcry's loyal defenders inflict 200% increased damage for each nearby member of the Trusted Guard.
      • Bound by Fate: Captain Dailcry and his trusted guard are bound by holy light, causing all four to share health while near each other. Captain Dailcry's presence increases the health of his nearby trusted guard by 1000%. (Mythic, Mythic+)
      • Elaena Emberlanz
        • Holy Radiance: Elaena Emberlanz channels the wrath of the light, inflicting 648657 Holy damage to all players every 1 sec for 8 sec.
        • Divine Judgment: Elaena Emberlanz calls down Divine Judgment on her current target, inflicting 4864576 Holy damage and increasing their damage taken by 25% for 8 sec.
        • Repentance: Elaena Emberlanz inflicts 2432465 Holy damage to a target player and incapacitates them for 8 sec.
      • Sergeant Shaynemail
        • Brutal Smash: Sergeant Shaynemail smashes the ground with powerful force, inflicting 10540683 Physical damage and stunning players with 8 yards for 3 sec.
        • Lunging Strike: Sergeant Shaynemail leaps at a nearby target, inflicting 2270301 Physical damage to players within 5 yards of the target and causing victims to bleed for an additional 1216233 Physical damage every 3 sec for 18 sec.
      • Taener Duelmal
        • Ember Storm: Taener Duelmal calls down burning embers that inflict 6486574 Fire damage to players within 3.5 yards of the impact locations.
        • Cinderblast: Taener Duelmal launches a devastating Cinderblast at a player, inflicting 2594630 Fire damage. The victim is stunned and suffers 1621644 Fire damage every 1 sec for 5 sec.
        • Fireball: Taener Duelmal launches a fireball at a target player, inflicting 1945972 Fire damage.

    Baron Braunpyke

    Distraught over the death of his brother, Baron Braunpyke has pledged his loyalty to Prioress Murrpray. The powerful knight now acts as her personal enforcer, protecting the secrets of the Priory from both outsiders and insufficiently loyal Arathi.

    • Overview: Baron Braunpyke bends the light to his will, hurling his [Castigator's Shield], and summoning [Hammers of Purity] from above.
      Upon reaching 100 Holy energy, Braunpyke empowers his abilities with [Vindictive Wrath].
      • Tanks:
        While empowered by [Vindictive Wrath] Baron Braunpyke's melee strike damage is increased.
        [Castigator's Shield] leaves [Castigator's Detonation] at the location of each player hit.
      • Damage Dealers:
        [Burning Light] will heal Braunpyke and damage those around him if not interrupted.
        [Castigator's Shield] leaves [Castigator's Detonation] at the location of each player hit.
      • Healers:
        The damage during [Vindictive Wrath] is greatly increased.
        [Castigator's Shield] leaves [Castigator's Detonation] at the location of each player hit.
    • Vindictive Wrath: Braunpyke empowers himself with the light, increasing his Physical damage by 100% and empowering each of his abilities.
    • Castigator's Shield: Braunpyke hurls a holy shield at several players, inflicting 2359314 Holy damage and reducing movement speed by 50% for 5 sec upon impact.
      While empowered by Vindictive Wrath, Castigator's Shield hits 5 players.
      • Castigator's Detonation: Holy energy erupts from the impact locations of Castigator's Shield, inflicting 5675752 Holy damage to players within 12 yards.
    • Burning Light: Braunpyke unleashes a burst of holy light, inflicting 3243287 Holy damage to all players and an additional 3243287 Holy damage every 2 sec for 12 sec. The burst of light also heals Braunpyke for 10% of his maximum health.
      While empowered by Vindictive Wrath, the cast time of Burning Light is reduced.
    • Hammer of Purity: Braunpyke calls searing light down on a player's location, inflicting 4864577 Holy damage to players within 3 yards. Hammers then spiral outwards from the impact location, inflicting 4864577 Holy damage to players in their path.

      While empowered by Vindictive Wrath, Hammer of Purity hits 5 players.
    • Sacrificial Pyre: Braunpyke creates a burning pyre containing 3 charges that last for 30 sec. Each time a player comes into contact with the pyre, a charge is consumed, the player is affected by Sacrificial Flame and causing the pyre to erupt in holy energy, inflicting 10 damage to all players.
      While empowered by Vindictive Wrath, Sacrificial Pyre has 5 charges. (Mythic)
      • Sacrificial Flame: A searing flame that inflicts 648657 Holy damage every 1 sec for 15 sec. This effect stacks. (Mythic)

    Prioress Murrpray

    Prioress Haelsey Murrpray directed the building of the Priory of the Sacred Flame to contemplate the sacred star Beledar. Insight into the emperor's vision has proved elusive. Instead, she has discovered secrets of the Sacred Flame that will bring them victory over their enemies in Hallowfall, and at home in the empire.

    • Overview: Prioress Murrpray harnesses the power of [The Sacred Flame] to vanquish her foes, burning them with its purifying beam of light.
      Upon reaching 50% health, Murrpray retreats to the Sacred Flame and shrouds herself with a [Barrier of Light] while calling upon her devoted followers.
      • Tank:
        Prioress Murrpray inflicts increased Holy damage while affected by [Inner Fire].
        Arathor Devoted inflict Holy damage to nearby players while affected by [Overwhelming Power]. (Mythic, Mythic+)
      • Damage Dealers:
        Prioress Murrpray inflicts increasing Holy damage while [Barrier of Light] holds.
        Arathor Devoted inflict Holy damage to nearby players while affected by [Overwhelming Power]. (Mythic+, Mythic)
      • Healer:
        Prioress Murrpray inflicts increased Holy damage while affected by [Inner Fire].
        Prioress Murrpray inflicts increasing Holy damage while [Barrier of Light] holds. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Prioress Murrpray
      • Barrier of Light: Upon reaching 50% health, Murrpray protects herself with a shield of light that absorbs the next 35320304 damage and providing immunity to interrupt effects.
        • Embrace the Light: Murrpray channels the power of the Sacred Flame, inflicting 162164 Holy damage to all players every 1 sec. This effect grows in intensity over time.
      • The Sacred Flame: Murrpray calls down a ray of searing fire from the Sacred Flame that inflicts 2270301 Holy damage every 1 sec to players caught in its path, and leaves behind a pool of Sanctified Ground.
        • Sanctified Ground: The fires left by the Sacred Flame inflict 2270301 Holy damage every 1.5 sec to players caught within them.
      • Blinding Light: Murrpray unleashes a dazzling light that inflicts 1621644 Holy damage to all players. Players looking in Murrpray's direction suffer an additional 1945972 Holy damage and are disoriented by the Blinding Light for 4 sec. (Mythic, Mythic+)
      • Inner Fire: The Sacred Flame empowers Murrpray, increasing her Holy damage by 50% for 10 sec. While empowered, Murrpray radiates 4 Holy damage to all players every 5.2 sec.
      • Holy Flame: Murrpray calls down a ray of light at a player's location, inflicting 4054109 Holy damage and an additional 1621644 Holy damage every 1 sec to any players within 7.5 yards of the impact location.
      • Holy Smite: Murrpray smites her current target, inflicting 2756794 Holy damage.
    • Arathi Neophyte
      • Overwhelming Power: [Empowered by the Sacred Flame, increasing damage done by 50% and inflicting 486493 Holy damage to players within 4 yards every 1 sec.
        The overwhelming power of the Sacred Flame drains 4% health every 1 sec.]

    Achievements for Priory of the Sacred Flame in the War Within

    There are four Achievements that can be earned in Priory of the Sacred Flame.
    • Prioria della Fiamma Sacra
      Defeat Prioress Murrpray in Priory of the Sacred Flame.
    • Eroica: Prioria della Fiamma Sacra
      Defeat Prioress Murrpray in Priory of the Sacred Flame on Heroic difficulty or higher.
    • Mitica: Prioria della Fiamma Sacra
      Defeat Prioress Murrpray in Priory of the Sacred Flame on Mythic or Mythic Keystone difficulty.
    • Eroe delle chiavi: Prioria della Fiamma Sacra
      Complete Priory of the Sacred Flame at Mythic Level 10 or higher, within the time limit.
      Feat of Strength | Reward: Teleport to Priory of the Sacred Flame
