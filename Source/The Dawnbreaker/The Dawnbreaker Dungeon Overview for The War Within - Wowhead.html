The Dawnbreaker Dungeon Overview for The War Within

Di iMX3 Aggiornato: 12/08/2025
This guide to Alba Infranta includes the dungeon location and how to get there, a boss list with abilities, and all relevant loot that drops in the dungeon, as well as all achievements that can be earned.

For information about Mythic+ dungeons, including which dungeons are active in this seasonal rotation and strategy guides for each, check out our Mythic+ Season Guide.

The War Within Season 3 Mythic+ GuideThe War Within Season 3 Mythic+ Guide


The Dawnbreaker is a three boss dungeon located in Hallowfall. The Arathi flagship is finally complete. This new flagship comes with enough firepower to turn the war against Azj-Kahet, but outside forces are attempting to sabotage it!
The Arathi have completed construction of their flagship, the Dawnbreaker. A new warship design with the firepower to turn the tide in this endless war against Azj-Kahet. The ceremonial ship launching is about to begin and General Steelstrike has requested the appearance of heroic outsiders to witness this historic launch.

The Dawnbreaker Mythic+ Guide Cheat Sheet for the War Within

How to get to The Dawnbreaker in the War Within

The Dawnbreaker is located in Hallowfall at Tenir's Ascent. Tenir's Ascent is the dock for The Dawnbreaker flagship. The dungeon entrance can be found at /way 54.7 62.7, with the flightpoint for Tenir's Ascent being at /way 54.0 62.0. This flagship is quite impressive in size, and your dungeon entrance is in the castle walls that surround the dock.

Dungeon Map for The Dawnbreaker in the War Within

The Dawnbreaker dungeon is a three boss dungeon that involves the entire city of Mereldar. The dungeon starts with boarding the Dawnbreaker and taking on Speaker Shadowcrown. Once she is defeated, players will fly into the city of Mereldar and face Anub'ikaj. Lastly, players will return to the Dawnbreaker where they will face Rasha'nan.

There are a few places where Skyriding is required. Make sure you have your Skyriding tree leveled, and Skyriding active, before taking on this dungeon!

Defending The Dawnbreaker: Summoning Speaker Shadowcrown

Before taking on Speaker Shadowcrown, there are a few Nerubian airships that will try to web the ship in place and destroy it. At this point, players will receive Luce Radiosa, a debuff that allows players to Skyride during the dungeon instance. Simply fly to each opposing Nerubian airship and take out the enemies aboard. Once all ships have been removed, Speaker Shadowcrown will appear back on the Dawnbreaker for players to face.

Neutralize Anub'ikkaj's Lieutenants!

After defeating Speaker Shadowncrown, players are instructed to fly into the city of Mereldar where they will face off against Anub'ikkaj. Before jumping right into this boss fight, it is wise to remove the lieutenants located throughout the city. Anub'ikkaj has a buff, Vigore Potenziato, which grants the boss +30% damage and +50% maximum health for every lieutenant still alive. Remove the lieutenants to weaken his power, then take him on!

There are three lieutenants to defeat:
  • Deathscreamer Iken'tak
  • Ixkreten the Unbreakable
  • Ascendant Vis'coxria
The location of each of the lieutenants is marked on the map above with a red pin. Defeating each lieutenant will weaken Anub'ikkaj, making him much easier to defeat!

Dungeon Waystones for The Dawnbreaker

There is one Waystone that serves as the dungeon checkpoint for The Dawnbreaker. This Waystone is located on the Dawnbreaker itself in the Captain's quarters of the ship. Releasing at any point in the dungeon will respawn you back on the Dawnbreaker.

Achievements for The Dawnbreaker in the War Within

There are four Achievements that can be earned in The Dawnbreaker.
  • Alba Infranta
    Defeat Rasha'nan in The Dawnbreaker.
  • Eroica: Alba Infranta
    Defeat Rasha'nan in The Dawnbreaker on Heroic difficulty or higher.
  • Mitica: Alba Infranta
    Defeat Rasha'nan in The Dawnbreaker on Mythic or Mythic Keystone difficulty.
  • Eroe delle chiavi: Alba Infranta
    Complete The Dawnbreaker at Mythic Level 10 or higher, within the time limit.
    Feat of Strength | Reward: Teleport to The Dawnbreaker
