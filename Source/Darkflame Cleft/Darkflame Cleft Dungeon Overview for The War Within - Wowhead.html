Darkflame Cleft Dungeon Overview for The War Within

Di iMX3 Aggiornato: 24/02/2025
This guide to Faglia di Fiammoscura includes the dungeon location and how to get there, a boss list with abilities, and all relevant loot that drops in the dungeon, as well as all achievements that can be earned.

Darkflame Cleft is featured in The War Within Mythic+ Season 2

For information about Mythic+ dungeons, including which dungeons are active in this seasonal rotation and strategy guides for each, check out our Mythic+ Season Guide.

The War Within Season 3 Mythic+ GuideThe War Within Season 3 Mythic+ Guide


Once a prosperous mine, Faglia di Fiammoscura is a four boss dungeon located in the northern part of ProfonditÃ  Risonanti. Now swarmed with kobolds, these mines suffer under the rule of the terrible Candle King.
Darkflame Cleft was once a prosperous earthen mine, but eventually, that prosperity ended. Earthen Machine Speakers moved out, and not long thereafter, kobolds swarmed in. The workers were soon oppressed by a tyrannical Candle King, a tall, bulky brute who towered over his subjects. Obvious wealth was extracted long ago. The real opportunity here is to defeat the last of the king's horrendous henchmen and liberate the intimidated kobolds within.

    How to get to Darkflame Cleft in the War Within

    Darkflame Cleft is located in The Ringing Deeps at /way 59.0 21.0. This location is northeast of the Gundargaz flightpoint located at /way 47.5, 34.2. Players will look for the entrance to a mine that is decorated with lit candles, the summoning stone is directly outside as well.

    Changes to Darkflame Cleft for Season 2 Mythic+

    Darkflame Cleft is featured in the Mythic+ rotation for Season 2 of The War Within. There are a few significant changes coming to the dungeon that will be detailed below.

    Blazikon Boss Changes

    Blazikon has received damage nerfs to its extinguishing abilities. While they will still extinguish candles, players will have a much easier job surviving the abilities themselves.

    • Respiro di Spegnimento damage reduced by 20%.
    • Raffica Estinguente:
      • Cast time increased to 4 seconds.
      • Now casts a Tornado that moves in a straight line towards players' locations, which can be dodged. (This was previously a Fixate that would follow players).
      • Damage reduced by 33%.
      • No longer stuns.

    Candle King Boss Changes

    The Candle King's Darkflame Pickaxe ability now has a shorter cast time, giving players less wiggle room to position near wax statues. Plan accordingly!

    • Piccone di Fiammoscura cast time reduced to 4 seconds (was 6 seconds).

    The Darkness Boss Changes

    The damage of the Shadowblast ability has been reduced, allowing for better player survivability.

    • Detonazione d'Ombra damage reduced by 37.5%

    Full List of Changes

    To see the full list of changes coming with the Darkflame Cleft dungeon, check out our datamining post below!

    Dungeon Map for Darkflame Cleft in the War Within

    Darkflame Cleft is a one-floor vast mine full of kobolds, candle elementals, and everything creepy that comes with abandoned mines. Players will first take on Ol' Waxbeard, followed by Blazikon, then The Candle King himself. But even with The Candle King gone, The Darkness remains, being the reason kobolds keep candles lit and huddle near campfires. It is up to players to flee the mines while something evil lurks in the shadows.

    THE DARKNESS WAITS

    The Candle King is defeated and the kobolds are free to escape! But something evil lurks where light cannot be found. This next section will cover how to progress through the last parts of the mines and take on the final boss of Darkflame Cleft: L'OscuritÃ .

    Once The Candle King is defeated, there will be a minecart with a large candle atop it that players can use to traverse through the dark parts of the mines. In this mini-game, players will need to push the cart through the dark mines while keeping small OscuritÃ  Brulicante adds from extinguishing the candlelight on the minecart.

    In this minecart mini-game, darkness shrouds the area and going too far away from the cart's light will give players a debuff named Ombre Asfissianti reducing all damage and healing done by 99%.



    Players will need to push this cart through the dark while keeping the candle lit!


    Along the way, there will be small candles that players can pick up and bring back to the minecart to keep the large candle flame lit. Stepping away from the cart to grab small candles will give players the Ombre Asfissianti debuff mentioned above. If you're going into the darkness to grab candles, make it quick!

    This process repeats through a few sections of the mines: Keep pushing the cart, defeat small OscuritÃ  Brulicante adds along the way, and keep the large candle flame atop the minecart lit at all times!


    Example of small candle


    Hunters can use their Bengala at any point in the Darkness and it will remove the Ombre Asfissianti debuff!

    During L'OscuritÃ  boss fight, the Ombre Asfissianti debuff is still present. Hunters can use their Bengala anytime they step into darkness during the fight and it will remove the debuff!


    After traversing through the dark mines, players will come upon a pile of dynamite that the kobolds find to be a perfect tool for a rocket boost out of here. Players will load their hands with dynamite and jump onto the cart. At this point, L'OscuritÃ  itself will begin chasing after the cart. Players will need to use the extra dynamite they found to throw at the boss in order to stun it. If successful, players will boost over a broken part of the track and land on the other side where they are finally able to face L'OscuritÃ  boss fight!


    Keep the Darkness away with Dynamite and escape!

    Darkflame Cleft Bosses in the War Within

    Ol' Waxbeard

    Howdy! Sit a spell. Hear about Ol' Waxbeard. Kobolds respect this old-timer for tamin' Wick, the rowdiest mole in creation! Waxbeard's seen folks come n' go. Now Candle King's in charge. That don't change nothin'! Ya still need to stay off his property! Why are here? Go on! Get!

    • Overview: Ol' Waxbeard summons waves of kobolds and deadly minecarts that run over all units. He marks priority targets for his allies with [Luring Candleflame] and spurs Wick into a [Reckless Charge] towards a distant player, devastating players and kobolds alike in his path.
      • Tanks:
        Menial Laborers quickly become a threat as they survive, due to [Crude Weapons].
        Ol' Waxbeard's [Rock Buster] causes the target to take increased Physical damage for a brief period.
      • Healers:
        [Luring Candleflame] draws all Menial Laborers to focus the target.
        Ol' Waxbeard's [Reckless Charge], the resulting [Cave-In], and speeding minecarts all instantly kill Menial Laborers.
      • Damage Dealers:
        Menial Laborers quickly become a threat as they survive, due to [Crude Weapons].
        Ol' Waxbeard's [Reckless Charge], the resulting [Cave-In], and speeding minecarts all instantly kill Menial Laborers.
    • "Kol"-to-Arms: Ol' Waxbeard calls Menial Laborers into battle on foot or by minecart.
      • High Speed Collision: The minecart slams into anything in its path knocking back and inflicting 5675752 Physical damage to players it strikes.
        Fatal to Menial Laborers.
      • Menial Laborers: Menial Laborers will fixate random nearby players when they enter the fray.
        • Crude Weapons: Menial Laborers' attacks inflicts 25946 Physical Damage every 1 sec for 5 sec. This effect stacks.
    • Underhanded Track-tics: Menial Laborers sneak a dynamite laden cart onto the nearby tracks. If it is not destroyed within 20 sec, it explodes inflicting 35 Fire damage to all players with an additional 20 Fire damage every 1 sec for 20 sec. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Reckless Charge: Ol' Waxbeard commands Wick to charge towards the furthest player. This attack knocks back and inflicts 6486574 Physical damage to all players and triggers a Cave-In at the end of the charge.

      Fatal to Menial Laborers.
      • Cave-In: Ol' Waxbeard's charge reverberates through the cave, dropping boulders above each player after a short delay. Boulders inflict 25 Nature damage within 4 yards.
        Fatal to Menial Laborers.
    • Luring Candleflame: Waxbeard throws a fresh lit candle at a random player. The burning wax inflicts 2 Fire damage every 0.5 sec and causes all Menial Laborers to fixate on that target for 10 sec.
    • Rock Buster: Ol' Waxbeard pierces his current target's armor with their pickaxe, inflicting 10865011 Physical damage and increases Physical damage taken by 25% for 6 sec.

    Blazikon

    Living flame hungers for fuel. Blazikon burns eternal in Darkflame Cleft, simmering with malice and rage. Loyal kobold attendants worship here, for his incendiary realm is surrounded by dire darkness. Fanatic kobolds would gladly sate this monstrosity's hunger by sacrificing... you.

    • Overview: Blazikon creates flames using [Wicklighter Barrage] to seek players.
      Upon reaching 100 energy, he will blast an [Enkindling Inferno] aimed at all unlit candles.
      • Tanks:
        Blazikon assaults all players with [Blazing Storms] when there are no players are in melee combat.
        [Enkindling Inferno] blasts flames towards every unlit candle, setting them ablaze.
      • Damage Dealers:
        [Wicklighter Barrage] ignites any candles that are hit by it.
        [Extinguishing Gust] will extinguish any candle it reaches.
        [Enkindling Inferno] blasts flames towards every unlit candle, setting them ablaze. (Mythic, Mythic+)
      • Healers:
        [Wicklighter Barrage] ignites any candles that are hit by it.
        [Extinguishing Gust] will extinguish any candle it reaches.
        [Enkindling Inferno] blasts flames towards every unlit candle, setting them ablaze. (Mythic, Mythic+)
    • Enkindling Inferno: Blazikon unleashes a fiery burst towards each extinguished candle, reigniting that candle and inflicting 5677293 Fire damage to all players in its path.

      The inferno sears all players, inflicting 1 Fire damage every 1 sec for 8 sec. This effect stacks.
    • Wicklighter Barrage: Blazikon hurls a torrent of flames at players, inflicting 3243287 Fire damage to players within 6 yards of each impact.
      The flames of Wicklighter Barrage ignite candles.
    • Extinguishing Gust: Blazikon forms gusting winds toward target players. Tornadoes that reach players inflict 1621644 Nature damage.
      Tornadoes extinguish candles they touch. (Mythic, Mythic+)
    • Incite Flames: Blazikon causes all lit candles to surge with incandescent heat and spread burning patches of embers. These flames inflict 2918958 Fire damage every 1 sec to players standing within.
    • Dousing Breath: Blazikon summons strong winds, inflicting 3243287 Nature damage to all players and extinguishing all candles.
    • Blazing Storms: Blazikon invokes the power of flames when no players are in melee combat, inflicting 4864931 Fire damage to all players.

    The Candle King

    Candle King was the tyrannical ruler of a kobold kingdom. His subjects toiled in his mines until an outlander from afar stole his crown and deposed him. Now he's fled deeper within Darkflame Cleft, seething with rage and threatening to return. Snuff his flame once and for all.

    • Overview: The Candle King begins by making waxen statues that burn his foes. He also uses the power of darkflame to imbue his abilities, assaulting players with [Throw Darkflame] and [Darkflame Pickaxe].
      • Tanks:
        [Eerie Molds] cause Fire damage to all players as long as they persist.
        [Darkflame Pickaxe] inflicts high damage and knocks back the first target it strikes.
      • Damage Dealers:
        [Eerie Molds] cause Fire damage to all players as long as they persist.
        [Darkflame Pickaxe] inflicts high damage and knocks back the first target it strikes.
      • Healers:
        [Eerie Molds] cause Fire damage to all players as long as they persist.
        [Darkflame Pickaxe] inflicts high damage and knocks back the first target it strikes.
    • Eerie Molds: The Candle King forms several wax statues of players. Each statue inflicts 1 Fire damage to all players every 2 sec as long as it survives.
      • Cursed Wax: Evil darkness flows from each wax statue. A player coming within 2 yards of a statue causes the wax to flow from it and encase the player completely. This effect stuns the target for 20 sec. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Darkflame Pickaxe: The Candle King readies a pickaxe to throw at a target. The pickaxe collides with the first target it hits, inflicting 29 Shadow damage and knocking them back. If it strikes a wax statue, it instead shatters it.
    • Paranoid Mind: The Candle King amplifies players' fear of the dark, causing them to flee in fear for 4 sec.
    • Throw Darkflame: The Candle King marks random players. After a short time, he hurls the shadowy flames at them, inflicting 16 Shadow damage to targets within 5 yards and absorbing the next 25 healing on them. These flames are strong enough to melt wax creations.
      • Molten Wax: When a wax statue melts down, it forms a pool of molten wax for 45 sec. Standing within inflicts 1945972 Fire damage every 1 sec.

    The Darkness

    What terrors lurk in shadows? Why do kobolds keep candles lit? Tiny creatures huddle around campfires, telling tales of ancient evil. THE DARKNESS can be defeated but never destroyed. Instead, it inevitably returns in a new terrifying shape. Stay in the light. THE DARKNESS WAITS.

    • Overview: The Darkness enwreathes the area in smothering shadows, reducing combat abilities of anyone without a light source. It uses a variety of Shadow attacks to devour its foes and weaken the light.
      • Tanks:
        Players without [Candlelight] suffer the debilitating effects of [Smothering Shadows].
        [Umbral Slash] and [Shadowblast] will both damage the light if they strike it.
      • Damage Dealers:
        Players without [Candlelight] suffer the debilitating effects of [Smothering Shadows].
        The Darkness uses [Call Darkspawn], which approach the light and cast [Drain Light].
        [Umbral Slash] and [Shadowblast] will both damage the light if they strike it.
      • Healers:
        Players without [Candlelight] suffer the debilitating effects of [Smothering Shadows].
        [Umbral Slash] and [Shadowblast] will both damage the light if they strike it.
    • Smothering Shadows: Darkness enshrouds the area. All players without a light source have their damage and healing done reduced by 99%.
      • Rising Gloom: If the candle heat is depleted, The Darkness wreathes the area in gloom, inflicting 1297315 Shadow damage to all players and causing them to flee in fear for 1 sec. This also increases their damage taken by 10% and reduces their haste by 5% for 10 min. This effect stacks.
      • Candlelight: Remaining within the candle light protects the player from Smothering Shadows, but reduces the heat level of the candle over time. Players can pick up and move the candle light source.
      • Wax Lump: Hurl a lump of wax gathered from a dead Candlebearer to restore heat to your candle.
    • Eternal Darkness: The Darkness releases waves of pure shadow every 1 sec for 4 sec. Each wave inflicts 8 Shadow damage to all players and reduces the Candlelight's heat. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Call Darkspawn: The Darkness beckons multiple Wriggling Darkspawn to crawl out of the shadows.
      • Drain Light: Wriggling Darkspawns drain the flames from a candle, reducing its heat by 3 and inflicting 3 Shadow damage to players every 1 sec for 10 sec.
    • Umbral Slash: The Darkness readies its claws and slashes out in a line towards the Candlelight. The wave of darkness inflicts 6486574 Shadow damage to players hit and reduces the Candlelight's heat.
    • Shadowblast: The Darkness targets a player with a massive blast. After 6 sec, the shadows erupt, inflicting 4054109 Shadow damage to players within 10 yds. If this effect hits the Candlelight, it reduces its heat.

    Achievements for Darkflame Cleft in the War Within

    There are four Achievements that can be earned in Darkflame Cleft.
    • Faglia di Fiammoscura
      Defeat The Darkess in Darkflame Cleft.
    • Eroica: Faglia di Fiammoscura
      Defeat The Darkness in Darkflame Cleft on Heroic difficulty or higher.
    • Mitica: Faglia di Fiammoscura
      Defeat The Darkness in Darkflame Cleft on Mythic or Mythic Keystone difficulty.
    • Eroe delle chiavi: Faglia di Fiammoscura
      Complete Darkflame Cleft at Mythic Level 10 or higher, within the time limit.
      Feat of Strength | Reward: Teleport to Darkflame Cleft
