The Rookery Dungeon Overview for The War Within

Di iMX3 Aggiornato: 24/02/2025
This guide to Corveria includes the dungeon location and how to get there, a boss list with abilities, and all relevant loot that drops in the dungeon, as well as all achievements that can be earned.

The Rookery is featured in The War Within Mythic+ Season 2

For information about Mythic+ dungeons, including which dungeons are active in this seasonal rotation and strategy guides for each, check out our Mythic+ Season Guide.

The War Within Season 3 Mythic+ GuideThe War Within Season 3 Mythic+ Guide


The Rookery is a three boss dungeon located on the Isle of Dorn in the Capital City of Dornogal. The home for the stormrooks and Stormriders, the Rookery has suffered a terrible breach of void magic that has disturbed it from within. Allies corrupted by void magic are now your enemies. Drive out the corrupting forces within and cleanse the Rookery!
The Stormperch is not only the home and rookery of the stormrooks, but also the barracks and headquarters for Stormward Baelgrim and the Stormriders. For thousands of years, the Stormperch has stood unbreached by the earthen's enemies. Unfortunately, defense of the rookery is harder when the breach comes from within.

How to get to The Rookery in the War Within

The Rookery is located in Dornogal on the Isle of Dorn. The dungeon entrance is in Keeper's Terrace over towards the northwest part of the city at /way 31.5, 35.5. The flight point for Dornogal is located at /way 44.2, 55.5.

Changes to The Rookery for Season 2 Mythic+

The Rookery will be featured in the Mythic+ rotation for Season 2 of The War Within. There are a few significant changes coming with the dungeon that we will detail below.

Kyrioss Boss Changes

Kyrioss now has a few new abilities that make the fight more intense. For starters, there is now AoE damage during the rotating laser beam phase that will need to be healed through. Outside of the phase, a bunch of new abilities were added that players will need to dodge throughout the encounter, including slams, charges, and extra swirlies.

  • Torrente Fulminante now deals AoE damage every 3 seconds for 15 seconds and periodically strikes enemies with Grounding Bolt.
  • NEW Dardo Vincolante: Colpisce le posizioni bersaglio con un dardo vincolante, infliggendo 4848895 danni da natura a tutti i nemici entro 5 m.
  • NEW Tuono Dirompente: Evoca una tempesta che si schianta sui personaggi, infliggendo 3636671 danni a tutti i nemici entro 8 m dal punto d'impatto.
  • NEW Fulmine Selvaggio: Scatena un fulmine selvaggio contro i personaggi, infliggendo 6788452 danni da natura ai personaggi entro 5 m dal punto d'impatto.

Stormguard Gorren Boss Changes

The main change here is that the pool left behind by Lingering Void now lasts for 2 minutes. which will limit the amount of space that players have to move around during the encounter. A few abilities have also had their damage reduced, though Crush Reality now has an AoE component.

  • Corruzione Caotica's has been damage reduced by 50%.
  • Schiacciamento della RealtÃ  deals 25% less damage but hits all players.
  • Vuoto Persistente now last for 2 minutes (was 1 minute).

Voidstone Monstrosity Boss Changes

This boss has received a significant rework that changes large portions of the fight. Breaking the shield now stuns the boss for twice as long and increases the damage it takes by 100%. Voidstone Fragments now pose a greater threat as they can turn into dangerous Voidstone Awakened adds. Stormrider's Charge can now be used to remove Voidstone Fragments before they Awaken and wipe the group.

  • Vendetta della Tempesta stun now lasts for 20 seconds (was 10 seconds) and increases damage taken by 100%.
  • Voidstone Awakened's Sovraccarico di Corruzione damage increased by 25% and increases all damage done by 20% every 3 seconds (was 5%).
  • Sollevazione Terrestre Nulla
    • A new initial hit of damage has been added, which cannot be line of sighted (i.e. hiding behind a pillar will not prevent the damage).
    • Swirlies damage increased by 33%.
    • Swirly travel time increased to 4 seconds (was 2 seconds).
    • Now summons Voidstone Fragments.
  • Carica dei Cavalcatempesta
    • Can now target 4 players.
    • Now lasts for 9 seconds (was 6 seconds).
    • Now Increases movement speed by 50%.
    • Now detonates any Voidstone Fragments hit.

Dungeon Map for The Rookery in the War Within

The Rookery is a three boss dungeon with five floors. While this sounds complex, the progression through this dungeon is quite simple to follow. From the entrance, players will take a Stormrook across to Storm's Roost, where Kyrioss lies corrupted with void magic. After defeating Kyrioss, players will float down to the Stormrider Barracks and fight their way through corrupted mobs. The Barracks connect to the Crashing Storm Hall, where they will take on Guardia della Tempesta Gorren. Lastly, players are brought down to the Abandoned Mines where the MostruositÃ  della Pietra del Vuoto dwells.

Interactive Buff Available in The Rookery

After the Kyrioss boss fight, players will soar down to the next level using Stormrook feathers. While descending, there are Captured Stormrooks that players can dash into with Carica Fulminante. Dashing into a Captured Stormrook will free it giving the player Favore dei Corvi della Tempesta. This buff lasts 5 minutes and inflicts extra Nature damage every second while in combat.

Achievements for The Rookery in the War Within

There are four Achievements that can be earned in The Rookery.
  • Corveria
    Defeat Voidstone Monstrosity in The Rookery.
  • Eroica: Corveria
    Defeat Voidstone Monstrosity in The Rookery on Heroic difficulty or higher.
  • Mitica: Corveria
    Defeat Voidstone Monstrosity in The Rookery on Mythic or Mythic Keystone difficulty.
  • Eroe delle chiavi: Corveria
    Complete The Rookery at Mythic Level 10 or higher, within the time limit.
    Feat of Strength | Reward: Teleport to The Rookery.
