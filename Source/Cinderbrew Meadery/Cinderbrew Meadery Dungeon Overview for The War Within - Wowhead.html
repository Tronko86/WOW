Cinderbrew Meadery is a 4 boss dungeon located on the Isle of Dorn. The Cinderbrew Mead is a staple of Earthen culture, where brew is crafted to be enjoyed by all Earthen.
Under new management! The Cinderbrew Meadery is a focal point of Unbound earthen culture, and under the dotage of Wenbrandt, the meadery has crafted a fiery, potent brew enjoyed by all earthen. However, the meadery has been the victim of a hostile corporate acquisition by Goldie Baronbottom, a goblin entrepreneur, ushering in a new age of mass-produced Cinderbrew.

    How to get to Cinderbrew Meadery in the War Within

    The entrance to the Cinderbrew Meadery is located at /way 75.9 44.6, on the Isle of Dorn. The dungeon is directly east of the capital city of Dornogal.

    Changes to Cinderbrew Meadery for Season 2 Mythic+

    Cinderbrew Meadery is featured in the Mythic+ rotation for Season 2 of The War Within. There are a few significant changes coming to the dungeon that we will detail below.

    Brew Master Aldryr Boss Changes

    The only change that's currently been datamined is to the intermission, where Rowdy Patrons have received a slight damage increase to their Rowdy Yell ability. This is likely in an effort to ensure that players carry out the intermission mechanics correctly.

    • Urlo Turbolento damage increased by 200%

    Benk Buzzbee Boss Changes

    Benk Buzzbee has had the damage of some of his abilities reduced, which will ease up on the difficulty of the encounter.

    • Ala Volteggiante damage reduced by 38.5%.
    • Marinata al Miele damage reduced by 30%.

    I'pa Boss Changes

    Similar to Benk Buzzbee, I'pa has also received a nerf to the damage of some of its abilities.

    • Montante Basso damage reduced by 50%.
    • Riempimento damage and shield reduced by 50%.

    Dungeon Map for Cinderbrew Meadery in the War Within

    Cinderbrew Meadery is a one-floor dungeon that has a main bar room with wings splitting off to the east and west. The dungeon begins by taking on Maestro dell'Idromele Aldryr. Once he has been defeated, players can clear the two wings held by I'pa and Benk il Ronzante. Upon defeating Brew Master Aldryr, I'pa, and Benk Buzzbee, a Percorso Diretto will appear back in the main bar room that allows players to fly up to the last boss, Dora Bottepiena.

    Interactive Buffs Available in Cinderbrew Meadery

    Cinderbrew Meadery has two interactable buffs available. The first one, the Pentolone da Cucina, is off to the left side of the bar in the start of the dungeon. Players with Khaz Algar Cooking or Alchemy of at least Skill 25 can interact with this pot and gain an extra action button of Miele Appiccicoso. This Miele Appiccicoso can be used on any player or mob reducing their Haste by 15% and their movement speed by 80% for 9 seconds.

    Be careful not to hit your teammates with the Miele Appiccicoso this as it will give them the debuff!


    The next interactive buff in this dungeon is a Lanciafiamme found in the hallways splitting off from the bar. Picking up the Flamethrower will require Goblins, Gnomes, Mechagnomes, or Khaz Algar Engineers of at least Skill 25. The Flamethrower gives an extra action button spell, In Fiamme!. When channeled, In Fiamme! strikes enemies inflicting 1% of their maximum health every second for 4 seconds and disorients them for 5 seconds.

     

    Cinderbrew Meadery Bosses in the War Within

    Brew Master Aldryr

    Barkeep and brew master for the meadery, Aldryr has more than mead on tap. Anyone who messes with the Cinderbrew Meadery's new management messes with Aldryr, and he has many friends in low places.

    • Overview: Brew Master Aldryr harnesses the explosive power of Cinderbrew, assaulting players with [Blazing Belch] and [Throw Cinderbrew].
      Upon reaching 100 energy, Brew Master Aldryr begins [Happy Hour] and causes Thirsty Patrons to unleash a [Rowdy Yell].
      • Tanks:
        Thirsty Patrons inflict Physical damage to all players with [Rowdy Yell] until appeased.
        [Keg Smash] inflicts high Physical damage and forms pools of [Hot Honey].
      • Damage Dealers:
        Thirsty Patrons inflict Physical damage to all players with [Rowdy Yell] until appeased.
        [Blazing Belch] inflicts high Fire damage towards a player in a frontal cone.
      • Healers:
        Thirsty Patrons inflict Physical damage to all players with [Rowdy Yell] until appeased.
        [Throw Cinderbrew] inflicts significant Fire damage to targeted players.
    • Happy Hour: Upon reaching 100 energy, Brew Master Aldryr leaps behind the counter to pour Cinderbrew for Thirsty Patrons. While pouring, he Disregards players.

      On Mythic difficulty, Crawling Brawls occur when Happy Hour ends.

      • Thirsty Patron
        • Rowdy Yell: Thirsty Patrons slam their table, inflicting 486493 Physical damage to all players every 2 sec and reducing their haste by 25%.
          When given a mug of Cinderbrew, these patrons cease their tantrum.
      • Disregard: Brew Master Aldryr ignores all distractions until players serve his Thirsty Patrons, reducing all damage taken by 99%.
      • Crawling Brawl: A barroom brawl breaks out in a flurry of fists and thick heads for 2 min. Players coming into contact with the brawl are stunned and suffer 1621644 Physical damage every 1 sec for 3 sec. (Mythic, Mythic+)
    • Throw Cinderbrew: Brew Master Aldryr tosses hot Cinderbrew at players, inflicting 1297315 Fire damage every 1.5 sec for 9 sec and forming Hot Honey underneath.
      • Hot Honey: Scalding honey forms on the ground for 1 min, inflicting 1945972 Fire damage every 1.5 sec to players standing within and reducing their movement speed by 50%.
    • Blazing Belch: Brew Master Aldryr takes a swig of Cinderbrew and unleashes a fiery belch towards a player, inflicting 6486574 Fire damage to players in a frontal cone.
    • Keg Smash: Brew Master Aldryr smashes a keg into his current target, inflicting 10865011 Physical damage and knocking them back. The keg splinters, forming Hot Honey.
      • Hot Honey: Scalding honey forms on the ground for 1 min, inflicting 1945972 Fire damage every 1.5 sec to players standing within and reducing their movement speed by 50%.

    I'pa

    The product of an industrial accident, mead elemental I'pa is as angry about its existence as it is about its meadery being disturbed. It is the full-bodied embodiment of the fiery punch of cinderbrew mead, and will lay anyone who tangles with it out flat.

    • Overview: I'pa embodies Cinderbrew itself, assailing players with [Spouting Stout] and [Burning Fermentation]. Brew Drops cause [Fill 'Er Up] if they reach I'pa. Defeating them creates pools of [Oozing Honey] that shrink.

      • Tanks:
        [Bottoms Uppercut] inflicts high Physical damage and knocks the targeted player back.
        [Spouting Stout] creates Brew Drops and inflicts moderate Fire damage to all players.
      • Damage Dealers:
        [Spouting Stout] creates Brew Drops and inflicts moderate Fire damage to all players.
        Brew Drops cause [Fill 'Er Up] if they reach I'pa.
      • Healers:
        [Burning Fermentation] inflicts high Fire damage to afflicted players.
        [Spouting Stout] creates Brew Drops and inflicts moderate Fire damage to all players.
    • Spouting Stout: I'pa expels mead, inflicting 1297315 Fire damage to all players every 2 sec for 8 sec. Mead droplets fling outward, inflicting an additional 45 Fire damage to players within 3 yards.

      Some droplets form into Brew Drops.
      • Brew Drop: Brew Drops move towards I'pa upon forming. (Heroic, Normal)
        • Oozing Honey: Brew Drops ooze burning honey upon death, inflicting 1945972 Fire damage every 1.5 sec to players standing within and reducing their movement speed by 50%.

          This honey shrinks over time.
        • Frothy: Upon reaching 100 energy the Brew Drop's increases its movement speed by 200%.
      • Brew Drop: Brew Drops move towards I'pa upon forming. (Mythic, Mythic+)
        • Oozing Honey: Brew Drops ooze burning honey upon death, inflicting 1945972 Fire damage every 1.5 sec to players standing within and reducing their movement speed by 50%.

          This honey shrinks over time, causing the Brew Drop to reform once it dissipates.

          In Mythic difficulty, Brew Drops reform after their Oozing Honey dissipates.

        • Oozing Honey: Brew Drops ooze burning honey upon death, inflicting 1945972 Fire damage every 1.5 sec to players standing within and reducing their movement speed by 50%.

          This honey shrinks over time, causing the Brew Drop to reform once it dissipates.

          In Mythic difficulty, Brew Drops will reform one time after their Oozing Honey dissipates.

        • Frothy: Upon reaching 100 energy the Brew Drop's increases its movement speed by 200%.
    • Fill 'Er Up: I'pa consumes Brew Drops it touches, inflicting 20 Fire damage to all players and granting I'pa a shield that absorbs damage equal to 10% of its maximum health.
    • Burning Fermentation: I'pa slathers players in burning mead, inflicting 1783808 Fire damage every 2 sec for 16 sec.
    • Bottoms Uppercut: I'pa punches its current target with a fist full of brew, inflciting 10865011 Physical damage and knocking them back.

    Benk Buzzbee

    Benk's qualifications for beekeeping may be dubious, but he does seem to have a knack for controlling bees and streamlining honey-extracting operations. But, his tampering has limited the bees' freedom and sacrificed the mead's quality, and this cannot stand.

    • Overview: Benk Buzzbee distributes barrels of delicious honey during [Snack Time], attracting Ravenous Cinderbees.
      Defeated Cinderbees become docile enough to be mounted by players and will charge forward with a [Bee-Haw!] command.
      • Tanks:
        [Honey Marinade] drips off targeted players on expiration, creating pools of flaming honey that inflict Fire Damage.
        [Snack Time] barrels in the arena will periodically attract Ravenous Cinderbees.
      • Damage Dealers:
        At 1 HP, Ravenous Cinderbees become tame enough to be ridden by players.
        [Snack Time] barrels are destroyed by the impact of a Ravenous Cinderbee charging forward with a [Bee-Haw!]
      • Healers:
        [Fluttering Wing] inflicts moderate Nature damage to all players.
        Ravenous Cinderbees dart between random players with [Shredding Sting], afflicting them with a stacking Bleed debuff.
    • Snack Time: Benk Buzzbee summons barrels of delicious honey. The collision inflicts 50 Physical damage to players standing within 3 yards of the impact.
      The honey's aroma attracts a Ravenous Cinderbee every 30 sec.
      • Ravenous Cinderbee
        • Shredding Sting: The Cinderbee leaps to an enemy and lodges its stinger into them. The puncture wound bleeds 729740 Physical damage every 2 sec for 10 sec.
        • Tamed: Upon reaching 1% health, Ravenous Cinderbees become docile and can be ridden by players.
          After 40 sec the Ravenous Cinderbee perishes.
        • Bee-Haw!: The Ravenous Cinderbee musters the last of its strength to charge forward 50 yards before finally perishing.
          The force of the collision will destroy any Honey Barrels in its path.
    • Honey Marinade: Benk Buzzbee coats his current target in flammable honey. After 5 sec the player ignites, inflicting 35 Fire damage to players within 6 yards of their location.
      Flaming honey remains on the ground, inflicting 9 Fire damage every 0.5 sec to players standing within the effect.

      On Mythic difficulty, Ravenous Cinderbees standing within the effect will become Honey Gorged.

      (Mythic+, Mythic)
      • Honey Gorged: The Ravenous Cinderbee binges on the Honey Marinade, healing themselves for 294336 health every 1 sec.
        The honey's sugar increases their haste by 50% and causes them to become immune to crowd control effects. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Fluttering Wing: Benk Buzzbee commands Cindy to furiously beat her wings, summoning a small gust around her that pushes players away and inflicts 4 Nature damage to all players every 0.5 sec for 2 sec.
    • Honey Marinade: Benk Buzzbee coats his current target in flammable honey. After 5 sec the player ignites, inflicting 35 Fire damage to players within 6 yards of their location.
      Flaming honey remains on the ground, inflicting 9 Fire damage every 0.5 sec to players standing within the effect. (Heroic, Normal)

    Goldie Baronbottom

    Savvy businesswoman Goldie Baronbottom knows a good opportunity when she sees it--like the Cinderbrew Meadery. After barging in and swindling Wenbrandt out of business, Goldie mostly lets her cronies do the work. Even so, she's not afraid to roll up her sleeves and wallop do-gooders herself.

    • Overview: Goldie Baronbottom uses [Spread the Love!] to launch Cinderbrew Bombs into the area. Goldie's weapon attacks can set nearby Cimderbrew Bombs alight.
      Upon reaching 100 energy, Goldie Baronbottom casts [Let It Hail!] detonating any remaining Cinderbrew Bombs.
      • Tanks:
        [Let It Hail] detonates any remaining Cinderbrew Bombs.
        [Cindering Wounds] iinflicts heavy Fire damage over a short duration to all players for each Cinderbrew Bomb that detonates.
        [Cash Cannon] ignites nearby Cinderbrew Bombs, causing them to explode. (Mythic, Mythic+)
      • Damage Dealers:
        [Let It Hail] detonates any remaining Cinderbrew Bombs.
        [Cindering Wounds] iinflicts heavy Fire damage over a short duration to all players for each Cinderbrew Bomb that detonates.
        [Burning Ricochet] bounces to nearby players and Cinderbrew Bombs. (Mythic+, Mythic)
      • Healers:
        [Let It Hail] detonates any remaining Cinderbrew Bombs and inflicts heavy Physical damage to all players.
        [Cindering Wounds] iinflicts heavy Fire damage over a short duration to all players for each Cinderbrew Bomb that detonates.
        [Burning Ricochet] inflicts heavy Fire damage over time to afflicted players. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Spread the Love!: Goldie Baronbottom stomps the ground, causing several Cinderbrew Bombs to fall into the room.

      On Mythic difficulty, some Cinderbrew Bombs are replaced with Volatile Barrels.

      • Cinder-BOOM!: Cinderbrew Bombs detonate upon being struck by Goldie Baronbottom, erupting and sending out waves of fire that inflicts 6486574 Fire damage to players struck and knocks them back.

        On Mythic difficulty, detonating a Volatile Barrel afflicts all players with Cindering Wounds.

        • Cindering Wounds: Volatile Barrels explode causing all players to burn, inflicting 648657 Fire damage every 1.5 sec for 6 sec.
          This effect stacks. (Mythic+, Mythic)
    • Let It Hail!: Upon reaching 100 energy Goldie Baronbottom fires wildly into the air, detonating all remaining Cinderbrew Bombs and inflicting 7 Fire damage to all players every 1 sec for 5 sec.
    • Burning Ricochet: Goldie Baronbottom arcs a bullet off of a flipped coin, inflicting 1297315 Fire damage with an additional 648657 Fire damage every 1.5 sec for 6 sec.
      The burning bullet ricochets to other players and Cinderbrew Bombs within 5 yards. Any Cinderbrew Bombs hit will erupt with Cinder-BOOM!.
    • Cash Cannon: Goldie Baronbottom blasts players and Cinderbrew Bombs with gold coins, inflicting 6486574 Physical damage in a frontal cone and knocking back all players struck. Any Cinderbrew Bombs hit will erupt with Cinder-BOOM!.

    Achievements for Cinderbrew Meadery in the War Within

    There are four Achievements that can be earned in Cinderbrew Meadery.
    • Idromelificio Cinereo
      Defeat Goldie Baronbottom in Cinderbrew Meadery.
    • Eroica: Idromelificio Cinereo
      Defeat Goldie Baronbottom in Cinderbrew Meadery on Heroic difficulty or higher.
    • Mitica: Idromelificio Cinereo
      Defeat Goldie Baronbottom in Cinderbrew Meadery on Mythic or Mythic Keystone difficulty.
    • Eroe delle chiavi: Idromelificio Cinereo
      Complete Cinderbrew Meadery at Mythic Level 10 or higher, within the time limit.
      Feat of Strength | Reward: Teleport to Cinderbrew Meadery
