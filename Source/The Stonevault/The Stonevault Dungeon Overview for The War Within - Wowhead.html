The Stonevault Dungeon Overview for The War Within

Di iMX3 Aggiornato: 17/09/2024
This guide to Volta di Pietra includes the dungeon location and how to get there, bosses and boss abilities, and all relevant loot that drops in the dungeon, as well as all achievements that can be earned.

For information about Mythic+ dungeons, including which dungeons are active in this seasonal rotation and strategy guides for each, check out our Mythic+ Season Guide.

The War Within Season 3 Mythic+ GuideThe War Within Season 3 Mythic+ Guide


The Stonevault is a four boss dungeon located in The Ringing Deeps. The former leader of the Stonevault, Eirich, has become infected by void power and his transformation is terrifying. It is up to players to ensure he does not further his rise to power.
Machine Speakers maintain the Stonevault, a facility adjacent to their Hall of Awakening. Their former leader, High Speaker Eirich, was recently deposed after becoming infected with Void corruption. While Machinists labor and loyalists patrol, Eirich hides behind his most fanatic and corrupted followers. Sequestered behind titan tunnels, he has undergone a terrifying transformation. To ensure he never ascends to power again, your swift intervention is required.

The Stonevault Mythic+ Guide Cheat Sheet for the War Within

How to get to The Stonevault in the War Within

The entrance to the Stonevault is located at /way 46.8 9.4, in the most northern parts of The Ringing Deeps. The closest flight point for this dungeon is Gundargaz at /way 46.9 32.8. This facility takes up a majority of the northern part of The Ringing Deeps, with the dungeon entrance being on the main walkway of the facility.

Dungeon Map for The Stonevault in the War Within

The Stonevault has one floor with multiple sections breaking off from Central Processing. You will enter the dungeon and head to Central Processing to face off against E.D.N.A. Once defeated, players will have a choice between facing Skarmorak in The Earthblood Crucible, or the Master Machinists in The Singing Forge. Once these two bosses are defeated, the main doors to the South will open for players to fight Void Speaker Eirich.

Minecart Fast Travel to Central Processing

After defeating Skarmorak or the Master Machinists, look for a minecart track to open up! This is a convenient fast travel back to Central Processing instead of walking all the way back! This will be especially helpful for saving time during Mythic+ runs of this dungeon!


Look for Minecarts to ride back to Central Processing after defeating bosses!

Interactive Buff: Lingotto di Ferro Infuso

Throughout the Dungeon are a few anvils containing an Lingotto di Ferro Infuso. Warriors, Dwarves, and Khaz Algar Blacksmiths of at least Skill 25 can interact with these anvils to grant players the Energia di Ferro Infusa buff for 10 minutes. This grants players increased Versatility!


Look for Anvils located throughout the Dungeon for the Energia di Ferro Infusa buff!

      • Void Empowerment: Become empowered by the beyond,
        taking 132157 Shadow damage every 2 sec. Additionally players gain 50% increased healing, and 50% increased damage to Crumbling Shell for30 sec.

Abilities

  • Speaker Brokk
    • Activate Ventilation: Speaker Brokk calls to the ventilation system to aid him, causing them to launch Flaming Scrap to the surrounding area. Standing on these vents while they're active inflicts 1762092 Fire damage.
      • Exhaust Vents: The Speakers energy courses through the machinery beneath them, causing exhaust vents to expel flames, inflicting 1762092 Fire damage every 2 sec to players on the vents, and 4 Fire damage every 1 sec to all players.
        Every 0.5 seconds Flaming Scrap shoots to a destination leaving behind burning ground.
        • Flaming Scrap: Exhaust Vents expel flaming refuse, leaving behind a burning puddle inflicting 18 Fire damage every 1.5 sec.

          On Mythic difficulty, Flaming Scrap last substantially longer.

    • Molten Metal: Speaker Brokk sends liquid metal surging into targets, inflicting 1145360 Fire damage, and slowing them by 50% for 8 sec.
    • Scrap Song: Speaker Brokk calls to the metal surrounding him to coalesce into a cube, which he then sends surging across the room inflicting 80 Physical damage, and stunning players caught in its path for 3 sec.
      • Scrap Cube: Inflicts 80 Physical damage and stuns players caught in the path of the Scrap Cube for 3 sec.
  • Speaker Dorlita
    • Deconstruction: Speaker Dorlita relocates the Scrap Cube to the center platform, and charges energy into it. The cube explodes, inflicting 7055845 Fire damage and an additional 1761685 Fire damage every 3 sec to players within 0 yards of the cube.
      Additonally inflicts 1939219 Fire damage to all players.
      • Metal Splinters: Speaker Dorlita relocates the Scrap Cube to the center platform, and charges energy into it. The cube explodes, inflicting 7055845 Fire damage and an additional 1761685 Fire damage every 3 sec to players within 0 yards of the cube.
        Additonally inflicts 1939219 Fire damage to all players.
    • Molten Hammer: Speaker Dorlita imbues her mech suit with molten energy, and strikes her target inflicting 5286277 Volcanic damage.
    • Lava Expulsion: Speaker Dorlita fires am orb of lava from her mech's cannon towards a random target, inflicting 3083662 Fire damage and an additional 528628 Fire damage every 2 sec for 10 sec.

      On Mythic difficulty, Lava Expulsion removes Flaming Scrap that gets hit

Eirich has been deposed, his authority seized! Overwhelmed, he fled to the innermost sanctum of the Stonevault. Yet in his machinations, he succumbed to transformation: Void energy from a malfunctioning artifact corrupts his living stone! Mystical revelations now shatter his sanity.

Overview

High Speaker Eirich infects players with [Void Corruption], its damage ramping until it is removed.
  • Tanks:
    [Void Corruption's] damage will ramp until it is removed.
    Standing in the center of [Void Rift] will instantly kill a player.
  • Damage Dealers:
    [Void Corruption's] damage will ramp until it is removed.
    Standing in the center of [Void Rift] will instantly kill a player.
  • Healers:
    [Void Corruption's] damage will ramp until it is removed.
    Standing in the center of [Void Rift] will instantly kill a player.

Abilities

  • Void Rift: High Speaker Eirich opens rifts to the Void, pulling in nearby players, killing anyone within 0 yards of the center of the rift

    On Mythic difficulty, Void Rifts move to a new location upon consuming [Void Corruption].

  • Void Corruption: High Speaker Eirich infects players with void energy, inflicting 528628 Shadow damage every 3 sec until removed. The damage increases by 25% every 3.
    Moving within 0 yards of a Void Rift will remove Void Corruption.
  • Entropic Reckoning: High Speaker Eirich unleashes entropy on all enemies, inflicting 1321569 Shadow damage to all players within 8 yards of the points of impact and leaving a zone of Entropy.
    • Entropy: Creates a zone of Entropy, inflicting 440523 Shadow damage every 1 sec.
  • Unbridled Void: High Speaker Eirich fires a cone of void energy at a player, inflicting 3083662 Shadow damage to all players in his front arc within 45 yards.

Stonevault Mount

The Meccanotuta Malfunzionante has a chance to drop off of Oratore del Vuoto Eirich. Collecting this item will give players the Un robot in difficoltÃ  quest.

This quest requires players to bring the Meccanotuta Malfunzionante to Oratore Jurlax in Gundargaz. (/way #2214 47.0 32.4 Speaker Jurlax)

From there, players will need to earn three trinkets from specific dungeons in Khaz Algar:
  • Lanciatore di Ingranarang Velocizzato from Oratrice Dorlita (Machine Speakers) in The Stonevault.
  • Materializzatore di Barili Sinergico from Dora Bottepiena in Cinderbrew Meadery.
  • Bulino del Re delle Candele from Il Re delle Candele in Darkflame Cleft.
Once all three trinkets are collected, return back to Oratore Jurlax to receive the Meccanotuta della Volta di Pietra Mount!

Achievements for The Stonevault in the War Within

There are four Achievements that can be earned in The Stonevault.
  • Volta di Pietra
    Defeat Void Speaker Eirich in The Stonevault.
  • Eroica: Volta di Pietra
    Defeat Void Speaker Eirich in The Stonevault on Heroic difficulty or higher.
  • Mitica: Volta di Pietra
    Defeat Void Speaker Eirich in The Stonevault on Mythic or Mythic Keystone difficulty.
  • Eroe delle chiavi: Volta di Pietra
    Complete The Stonevault at Mythic Level 10 or higher, within the time limit.
    Feat of Strength | Reward: Teleport to The Stonevault
