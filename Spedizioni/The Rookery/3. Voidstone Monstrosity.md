# Voidstone Monstrosity — Boss 3

Amalgamation corrupted Stormriders con void power. Meccanica chiave: Void Shell (shield break), Null Upheaval (Voidstone Fragments spawn), Voidstone Awakened (exponential damage), Stormrider's Charge (Fragment detonation).

## Panoramica boss

**Tipo**: Shield Break + Add Management Boss  
**Meccanica chiave**: Void Shell (20% max HP shield), Null Upheaval (Voidstone Fragments), Voidstone Awakened (Corruption Overload stacking), Stormrider's Charge (Fragment detonation)  
**Difficoltà principale**: break shield fast, kill Voidstone Fragments prima Awakening (15 sec), gestire Unleash Corruption pools (M+), coordinate Stormrider's Charge

## Meccaniche chiave

1. **Void Shell** (Involucro del Vuoto)
   - Boss **encases** in void energy shell
   - Assorbe **20% max HP** boss come damage
   - **Protegge** boss da **Storm's Vengeance**
   - Break shield → Stormrider Vokmar lancia **Storm's Vengeance**
     - **Stun boss** per 20 sec (aumentato da 10 sec S2)
     - **+100% damage taken** (aumentato da +50% S2)
   - Strategia: **burst DPS** shield → massimizza damage window durante stun

2. **Null Upheaval** (Sollevazione Terrestre Nulla)
   - Boss smash platform
   - **Initial hit**: 2.10M Shadow damage raid-wide (S2: non line-of-sightable)
   - **Cascading explosions**: swirlies
     - **6.48M Shadow damage** in 7 yards
     - Travel time **4 sec** (aumentato da 2 sec S2)
     - Damage +33% (S2)
   - **Ejects Voidstone Fragments** attorno arena
   - Strategia: dodge swirlies, prepare Fragment management

3. **Voidstone Fragment** (Frammento Pietravuoto)
   - Spawned da **Null Upheaval**
   - **Reshape**: crystallize in Voidstone Awakened in **15 sec**
   - **CRITICAL**: devono essere **detonati** prima Awakening
   - Detonazione metodi:
     - **Stormrider's Charge** (primary method)
     - Focus DPS (fallback se non charge disponibile)

4. **Voidstone Awakened** (Pietravuoto Risvegliata)
   - Se Fragment **non killato** entro 15 sec
   - **Corruption Overload**: 
     - **1.13M Shadow damage** raid-wide ogni 3 sec
     - **+20% damage done** ogni tick (aumentato da +5% S2)
     - **STACKS** indefinitamente
   - **Wipe mechanic**: se Awakened vivo → exponential damage = wipe rapido
   - Strategia: **PREVENT spawning** → detonate Fragments sempre

5. **Stormrider's Charge** (Carica dei Cavalcatempesta)
   - Stormrider Vokmar marca **4 players** (aumentato da 3 S2)
   - Buff: **+50% movement speed** per 9 sec (aumentato da 6 sec S2)
   - Vokmar lancia **lightning hammer**
   - Hammer **detona Voidstone Fragments** entro 10 yards marked players
   - Strategia: players marcati → **run verso Fragments** → detona tutti

6. **Unleash Corruption** (Scatenare Corruzione)
   - Boss marca **2 nearby players**
   - **Unstable Corruption** debuff:
     - **324k Shadow damage/sec** per 15 sec
     - **Dispel/removal** → **Seeping Corruption pool** (M+/Mythic only)
   - **Seeping Corruption** pool:
     - Dura permanente (fino fine fight)
     - **1.62M Shadow damage/sec** se inside
   - **Normal/Heroic**: no pool creation
   - **M+/Mythic**: debuff removal crea pool
   - Strategia (M+): posiziona pools in **edge** → minimizza spazio polluted

7. **Oblivion Wave** (Onda dell'Oblio)
   - Unleash void energy wave verso **current tank**
   - **6.48M Shadow damage** in path
   - Tank mechanic: **tank swap** o defensive cooldown

8. **Entropy** (Entropia)
   - Se **no players in melee range**
   - Boss smash platform
   - **2.43M Shadow damage** raid-wide
   - Anti-kite mechanic

## Fasi del combattimento

### Cycle continuo:
1. **Void Shell phase**
   - DPS shield fino break
   - Storm's Vengeance → boss **stunned 20 sec + vulnerable**
   
2. **Null Upheaval**
   - Dodge swirlies
   - **Voidstone Fragments spawn**
   
3. **Stormrider's Charge**
   - 4 players marcati
   - **Run verso Fragments** → detona
   
4. **Unleash Corruption**
   - 2 players DoT
   - (M+) Posiziona pools quando expire
   
5. Repeat

## Strategie per difficoltà

### Normal/Heroic
- **Void Shell**: break → DPS burst window
- **Voidstone Fragments**: Stormrider's Charge detonation
- **Null Upheaval**: dodge swirlies
- **No Seeping Corruption pools**

### M+/Mythic
- **Void Shell**: break FAST (20 sec stun + 100% vuln critical)
- **Voidstone Awakened**: +20% damage stacking = wipe rapidissimo
  - Priority #1: **prevent spawning**
  - Stormrider's Charge timing = critical
- **Seeping Corruption pools**: posiziona **strategicamente**
  - Durano fino fine fight
  - Arena shrink progressivo
- **Null Upheaval**: swirlies +33% damage, travel 4 sec
- High coordination requirement

## Tips per ruolo

### Tank
- **Oblivion Wave**: face boss **away** da gruppo
- **Entropy**: mantieni **melee range** sempre
- **Void Shell**: use **defensive** se shield break slow
- **Positioning**: keep boss **lontano da Seeping Corruption pools** (M+)

### Healer
- **Unleash Corruption**: 324k/sec DoT per 15 sec su 2 players
- **Null Upheaval**: raid-wide 2.10M + swirlies spike healing
- **Voidstone Awakened**: se spawn → **massive healing check**
  - 1.13M raid-wide ogni 3 sec
  - Stacking +20% damage
  - Call for **Lust + defensive CDs** se happen
- **Oblivion Wave**: tank spike healing
- **(M+) Seeping Corruption**: evita dispel debuff in bad spots

### DPS
- **Priority assoluta**:
  1. **Voidstone Fragments** → prevent Awakened spawn
  2. **Void Shell** break → massimizza stun window
  3. Boss DPS
- **Stormrider's Charge**: 
  - Se marcato → **immediatamente run verso Fragments**
  - Coordinate con altri marked players
  - Detona TUTTI Fragments prima expire
- **Null Upheaval**: dodge swirlies
- **Seeping Corruption (M+)**: se hai debuff → **move to edge** prima expire
- **Void Shell break**: save burst CDs per stun window

## Errori comuni

- **Ignore Voidstone Fragments** → Awakened spawn = wipe
- **Stormrider's Charge players stand still** → Fragments non detonati
- **Poor Seeping Corruption positioning (M+)** → arena shrink incontrollato
- **Slow Void Shell break** → miss damage window
- **Face tank Oblivion Wave** → tank death
- **Kite boss** → Entropy spam = raid damage
- **Panic during Null Upheaval** → deaths da swirlies

## Cooldown usage

- **Bloodlust**: primo Void Shell stun window (massimizza damage + prevent Awakened early)
- **Raid defensives**: Null Upheaval, Voidstone Awakened emergency
- **Tank defensives**: Oblivion Wave, Void Shell phase
- **Healer CDs**: Voidstone Awakened spawn (emergency), Null Upheaval spike
