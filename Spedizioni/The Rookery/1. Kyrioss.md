# Kyrioss — Boss 1

Stormrook corrotto di Stormguard Gorren. Meccanica chiave: Lightning Torrent (rotating beams a 100 energia), Unstable Charge (dispel + discharge), Wild Lightning/swirlies (M+).

## Panoramica boss

**Tipo**: Energy-based Phase + Dispel/Positioning Boss  
**Meccanica chiave**: Lightning Torrent (4 rotating beams), Unstable Charge (dispellable charge), Wild Lightning swirlies (M+)  
**Difficoltà principale**: evitare rotating beams, discharge Unstable Charge in safe zone, dodge swirlies/charges (M+)

## Meccaniche chiave

1. **Lightning Torrent** (Torrente Fulminante)
   - **A 100 energia**: Kyrioss vola al centro arena
   - Emette **4 torrents of lightning** che ruotano
   - Toccare torrent → **12.97M Nature damage + 6 sec stun**
   - **Normal/Heroic**: solo beam damage
   - **M+/Mythic**: aggiunge **AoE raid-wide damage ogni 3 sec per 15 sec** + Grounding Bolt strikes
   - Strategia: **dodge beams** ruotando attorno ai edges arena

2. **Unstable Charge** (Carica Instabile)
   - Kyrioss marca **random player** con elettricità
   - Player ottiene **dispellable debuff**
   - **Dispel** → **Unstable Blast**: esplosione 3.24M Nature damage in AoE 6 yards
   - **SAFE DISCHARGE**: dispel player nel **central Stormheart tempest**
   - Errore comune: dispel fuori tempest = raid damage spike

3. **Grounding Bolt** (Dardo Vincolante) [M+/Mythic only]
   - Durante **Lightning Torrent phase**
   - **Swirlies**: strike target locations
   - **3.24M Nature damage** in 5 yards
   - Dodge swirlies durante beam rotation

4. **Wild Lightning** (Fulmine Selvaggio) [M+/Mythic only]
   - Unleash lightning **toward players**
   - **4.54M Nature damage** in 5 yards impact zone
   - **Swirlies** da evitare continuamente

5. **Crashing Thunder** (Tuono Dirompente) [M+/Mythic only]
   - Tempest crash su players
   - **2.43M damage** in 8 yards impact
   - Spread per minimizzare colpiti

6. **Lightning Dash** (Scatto Fulmineo)
   - Kyrioss leap verso location
   - **4.86M Nature damage** in 12 yards + knock-back
   - Evita melee range quando casta

7. **Chain Lightning** (Fulmine a Catena)
   - Arc fino a **3 players**
   - **4.05M Nature damage** per target
   - Spread minimizza chain jumps

8. **Stormheart** (Cuore della Tempesta)
   - **Central tempest** persistente
   - **Safe zone** per discharge Unstable Charge
   - Periodicamente lancia lightning strikes (35 Nature damage in 6 yards)

## Fasi del combattimento

### Phase 1: Normal Combat (0-100 energia)
- DPS boss
- Dodge **Wild Lightning, Crashing Thunder, Lightning Dash**
- **Unstable Charge**: player marcato → va in central tempest → **healer dispel**
- Evita **Chain Lightning** spread

### Phase 2: Lightning Torrent (100 energia)
- Kyrioss vola **al centro**
- **4 rotating lightning beams**
- **Dodge beams** ruotando attorno edge
- **M+/Mythic**: AoE damage ogni 3 sec + Grounding Bolt swirlies
- Fase dura ~15 sec
- Dopo fase → torna Phase 1

## Strategie per difficoltà

### Normal/Heroic
- Focus: **dodge Lightning Torrent beams**
- Unstable Charge: dispel in tempest
- Movement moderato

### M+/Mythic
- **Maggiore movement**: Wild Lightning, Grounding Bolt, Crashing Thunder swirlies
- **Lightning Torrent**: AoE healing check (raid-wide damage ogni 3 sec)
- **Priority**: dodge swirlies > DPS
- Bloodlust timing: pull o dopo primo Lightning Torrent

## Tips per ruolo

### Tank
- Posiziona boss **vicino center** per facilitare Unstable Charge discharge
- **Lightning Dash**: back off quando casta
- Durante Lightning Torrent: dodge beams come gruppo

### Healer
- **Unstable Charge**: dispel player quando in **central tempest**
- **Lightning Torrent (M+)**: raid-wide healing ogni 3 sec per 15 sec
- **Grounding Bolt/Wild Lightning**: spot heal swirly damage
- Pre-heal gruppo prima Lightning Torrent phase

### DPS
- **Priority**: dodge mechanics > DPS uptime
- **Unstable Charge**: se marcato → **vai in tempest** → call for dispel
- **Lightning Torrent**: non facetank beams (stun = DPS loss)
- **M+ swirlies**: constant movement, dodge Wild Lightning/Grounding Bolt

## Errori comuni

- **Dispel Unstable Charge fuori tempest** → raid damage spike
- **Facetank Lightning Torrent beams** → 6 sec stun = wipe risk
- **Stack durante Chain Lightning** → excessive chain damage
- **Ignore swirlies (M+)** → morte DPS/healer
- **Poor positioning** durante Lightning Dash → knock-back in bad spots
