# Skarmorak — Boss 2A

Golem infuso con Skardyn corruption. Meccanica chiave: Unstable Crash (collision con crystal raid-wide), crystal positioning, Void Discharge.

## Panoramica boss

**Tipo**: Crystal Management + Tank Swap Boss  
**Meccanica chiave**: Unstable Crash (charge + crystal collision = raid-wide), crystal positioning critical, Void Discharge (tank swap), Fortified Shell (shield)  
**Difficoltà principale**: posizionare crystal correttamente, evitare Unstable Crash collision, tank swap timing, break shield

## Meccaniche chiave

1. **Unstable Crash** (Impatto Instabile)
   - Skarmorak **charges** verso posizione target
   - Se **collision con crystal** durante charge:
     - **RAID-WIDE devastante** Shadow/Physical damage
     - Crystal shattered
     - Più crystal hit = più damage
   - **CRUCIALE**: posizionare crystal per **EVITARE** charge path
   - Se no crystal collision → solo target prende damage

2. **Crystalline Smash** (Impatto Cristallino)
   - Skarmorak slams terra
   - Crea **crystal** a posizioni fisse
   - Crystal persiste fino a shattered
   - **Positioning crystal**:
     - Crystal deve essere **lontano da boss charge path**
     - Se Unstable Crash hits crystal → raid-wide wipe risk

3. **Void Discharge** (Scarica del Vuoto)
   - Colpo pesante tank melee
   - Lascia **stacking debuff** su tank
   - Debuff aumenta Shadow damage taken
   - **TANK SWAP** quando stack troppo alti (2-3 stack)
   - Season 2/3: verificare stack count safe

4. **Fortified Shell** (Guscio Fortificato)
   - Skarmorak casta **shield** su sé stesso
   - Shield **assorbe damage** (damage reduction massive)
   - **Break shield** priority
   - Se shield persiste troppo → enrage risk

5. **Unstable Energy** (Energia Instabile)
   - Aura passiva che stack su tank
   - Aumenta damage taken progressivamente
   - Combina con Void Discharge debuff
   - Motivo principale tank swap

6. **Skardyn Fragments** (Frammenti Skardyn)
   - Quando crystal shattered → spawn **add fragments**
   - Add si muovono verso raid
   - Moderato damage se alive
   - **Cleave add** durante DPS boss

## Cosa interrompere / dispellare / evitare

- **Interrupt**: non necessario (no cast interrompibili critici)
- **Evitare**: 
  - **Crystal collision** durante Unstable Crash (raid-wide devastante)
  - **Charge path** (se sei target)
  - **Add melee range** se possibile (cleave)
- **Tank swap**: 
  - **Void Discharge** stacking debuff (2-3 stack)
- **Break priority**: 
  - **Fortified Shell** shield (damage reduction massive)

## Posizionamento e movimento

- **Tank**: 
  - **Posiziona boss** lontano da crystal
  - Quando Skarmorak cast **Unstable Crash**:
     - Identify charge target
     - Se sei target → **run perpendicular** to crystal
     - Charge path deve **evitare crystal** completamente
  - **Tank swap** su Void Discharge 2-3 stack

- **Raid**: 
  - **Spread medium** per evitare chain damage
  - Identify crystal positions dopo Crystalline Smash
  - **Stay lontano da charge path** durante Unstable Crash
  - Se charge target → **kite lontano da crystal**

- **Crystal positioning critica**:
  - Crystal spawn in posizioni fisse (Crystalline Smash)
  - **Boss deve essere tanked lontano da crystal**
  - Se Unstable Crash charge path verso crystal → tank move boss

- **Charge target**:
  - Skarmorak marca target per Unstable Crash
  - Target **runs in direction** che evita crystal
  - **Path DEVE essere crystal-free**

## Gestione cooldown

- **Raid CD**: se accidental crystal collision (Unstable Crash raid-wide)
- **Tank CD**: 
  - Su Void Discharge alto stack (se delayed swap)
  - Durante Fortified Shell phase (increased boss damage)
- **Lust/Heroism**: pull o ~50% HP (burn prima enrage)
- **DPS CD**: su Fortified Shell (break shield fast)

## Note per Tank

- **Void Discharge**:
  - **Stacking debuff** Shadow damage taken
  - **Tank swap** 2-3 stack (verify safe count)
  - Off-tank prepara taunt quando stack critical

- **Unstable Crash positioning**:
  - **CRITICO**: se sei charge target → **kite AWAY da crystal**
  - Charge path collision crystal = raid-wide wipe risk
  - Pre-plan escape route prima Unstable Crash cast

- **Fortified Shell**:
  - Shield massively reduces damage taken
  - Call out shield to raid (DPS burst)
  - Tank cooldown se shield persiste (boss still hitting hard)

- **Crystal awareness**:
  - Memorize crystal positions dopo Crystalline Smash
  - Tank boss in zona **crystal-free**
  - Se crystal troppo vicini → reposition boss

## Note per Healer

- **Unstable Crash**:
  - Se crystal collision → **RAID-WIDE massive** damage
  - Emergency raid CD se accident
  - Pre-shield raid se unsure positioning

- **Void Discharge**:
  - Tank stack damage progressivo
  - Monitor tank debuff count
  - Heavy heal active tank durante swap transition

- **Fortified Shell**:
  - Boss damage non ridotto verso tank durante shield
  - Continue heavy tank healing durante shield phase

## Note per DPS

- **Fortified Shell**:
  - **PRIORITY**: break shield fast
  - Lust/burst cooldown se disponibile
  - Shield persiste → enrage risk

- **Crystal positioning**:
  - **Non stare vicino crystal** durante Unstable Crash
  - Se sei charge target → **kite AWAY da crystal**
  - Crystal collision = raid-wide wipe risk

- **Skardyn Fragments**:
  - Add spawn da crystal shattered
  - **AoE cleave** add durante DPS boss
  - Non priority, ma kill se troppi

- **Positioning**:
  - Medium spread per evitare chain damage
  - Stay lontano da charge path
  - Ranged advantage (safe from charge/add)

## Strategia chiave

1. **Phase flow**:
   - Crystalline Smash → crystal spawn
   - Tank boss **lontano da crystal**
   - Unstable Crash → charge target kite AWAY da crystal
   - Void Discharge → tank swap 2-3 stack
   - Fortified Shell → DPS burst break shield

2. **Priority list**:
   - Evitare crystal collision (Unstable Crash)
   - Tank swap su Void Discharge stack
   - Break Fortified Shell shield priority
   - Cleave Skardyn Fragments add
   - Positioning crystal-aware sempre

3. **Common mistakes**:
   - Crystal collision durante Unstable Crash → raid wipe
   - No tank swap → active tank one-shot (Void Discharge stack)
   - Ignorare Fortified Shell → enrage
   - Tank boss troppo vicino crystal → charge collision risk
