# Master Machinists — Boss 2B

Duo Earthen machinists: Speaker Brokk e Speaker Dorlita. Meccanica chiave: duo boss simultaneo, Molten Metal (forge pool), Lava Cannon, shared health pool.

## Panoramica boss

**Tipo**: Duo Boss Fight + Forge Management  
**Meccanica chiave**: Speaker Brokk + Speaker Dorlita alive simultaneo, Molten Metal (pool forge damage), Lava Cannon (beam dodging), Scrap Song (shield), shared health pool  
**Difficoltà principale**: split tank duo boss, evitare Molten Metal zones, dodge Lava Cannon, interrupt casts, break shields

## Meccaniche chiave

1. **Duo Boss Mechanic** (Meccanica Doppio Boss)
   - **Speaker Brokk** + **Speaker Dorlita** alive simultaneo
   - **Shared health pool** (damage a uno = damage a entrambi)
   - **Devono essere killed insieme** (no uccidere uno solo)
   - Se uno muore prima → survivor enrage massivo
   - **Split tank** (un tank per boss)

2. **Molten Metal** (Metallo Fuso)
   - Speaker **slams forge**, creando **pool molten metal** a terra
   - Pool persiste lungo fight
   - **Pool damage**: alto Fire damage DoT se dentro
   - Più pool = meno spazio safe arena
   - **EVITA pool** sempre

3. **Lava Cannon** (Cannone Lava)
   - Speaker Dorlita casta **beam lava** verso target
   - Beam **sweep in arc** (rotazione)
   - **Alto Fire damage** se hit
   - **Dodge beam** movimento laterale
   - Telegraph visibile (pre-move)

4. **Scrap Song** (Canto Rottame)
   - Speaker Brokk casta **shield** su sé stesso o Dorlita
   - Shield **assorbe damage** massivo
   - **INTERRUPT** Scrap Song priority #1
   - Se shield up → DPS loss significativo
   - Interrompibile cast

5. **Heated Metallurgy** (Metallurgia Riscaldata)
   - Aura che aumenta Fire damage raid-wide
   - Stack progressivamente durante fight
   - **Healing pressure** aumenta nel tempo
   - Burn boss prima che stack troppo alto

6. **Exhaust Vents** (Sfiati Esausti)
   - Periodicamente arena rilascia **steam vents**
   - Vents danneggiano giocatori in zone
   - **Posizionamento**: evita vent zones
   - Riduce spazio safe (combina con Molten Metal pool)

7. **Blazing Crescendo** (Crescendo Ardente)
   - Cast raid-wide Fire damage
   - **Interrompibile**
   - Priority interrupt se Scrap Song già interrotto
   - Se cast completo → alto raid damage

## Cosa interrompere / dispellare / evitare

- **Interrupt priority**: 
  1. **Scrap Song** (shield massivo) — PRIORITY #1
  2. **Blazing Crescendo** (raid-wide damage)
- **Evitare**: 
  - **Molten Metal pools** (Fire DoT devastante)
  - **Lava Cannon beam** (dodge lateralmente)
  - **Exhaust Vent zones** (steam damage)
- **Split target**: 
  - **NO** — shared health pool (damage entrambi equally)

## Posizionamento e movimento

- **Tank 1 (Speaker Brokk)**: 
  - Tank Brokk **separato da Dorlita**
  - Posiziona **lontano da Molten Metal pools**
  - Face Brokk lontano da raid (possibile frontal)
  - Swap se debuff stack troppo alto

- **Tank 2 (Speaker Dorlita)**: 
  - Tank Dorlita **altro lato arena**
  - Posiziona per **Lava Cannon beam** non colpisce raid
  - Face Dorlita in direzione safe
  - Coordinate con raid per beam dodge

- **Raid**: 
  - **Medium spread** tra due boss
  - **Evita Molten Metal pools** sempre
  - Quando **Lava Cannon** cast → **move perpendicular** to beam
  - **Exhaust Vents** spawn → reposition fuori zones

- **Melee DPS**:
  - Split tra Brokk/Dorlita (shared health = target non importa)
  - Evita Molten Metal pool
  - Dodge Lava Cannon se vicino Dorlita

- **Ranged DPS**:
  - Target either boss (shared health)
  - Medium range per evitare Lava Cannon
  - Interrupt rotation (Scrap Song priority)

## Gestione cooldown

- **Lust/Heroism**: pull o ~40% HP (burn prima Heated Metallurgy stack troppo alto)
- **Raid CD**: su Heated Metallurgy alto stack (Fire damage raid-wide aumentato)
- **Tank CD**: su melee combo + Molten Metal proximity damage
- **DPS CD**: pull (maximize burn, shared health pool)

## Note per Tank

- **Duo split**:
  - Tank 1 → Speaker Brokk
  - Tank 2 → Speaker Dorlita
  - **Separate boss** (non stack insieme)

- **Molten Metal awareness**:
  - Pool spawn da forge slam
  - **Reposition boss** fuori pool immediatamente
  - Pool persiste → arena progressivamente più piccola

- **Lava Cannon**:
  - Dorlita tank: posiziona boss per **beam non hits raid**
  - Face Dorlita verso wall o zona safe
  - Dodge beam se sei in path

- **Scrap Song**:
  - Se Brokk cast shield → call interrupt
  - Shield su boss = massive DPS loss
  - Coordinate interrupt rotation con DPS

## Note per Healer

- **Heated Metallurgy**:
  - Fire damage raid-wide **stack progressivamente**
  - Healing pressure aumenta durante fight
  - Mana management critico (long fight = oom risk)

- **Molten Metal**:
  - Giocatori in pool → **massive Fire DoT**
  - Call out se vedi qualcuno in pool
  - Emergency heal se accident

- **Lava Cannon**:
  - Beam hit → alto Fire damage spike
  - Pre-shield/heal target se unsure dodge

- **Blazing Crescendo**:
  - Se cast completo → raid-wide damage spike
  - Pre-shield raid se interrupt fail

## Note per DPS

- **Interrupt rotation**:
  - **Scrap Song** — PRIORITY #1 interrupt
  - Coordinate rotation (multiple interrupters)
  - Se shield up → massive DPS loss

- **Target selection**:
  - **Shared health pool** → target either boss
  - Preferenza: focus uno per melee/ranged split
  - DPS distribution non importa (shared HP)

- **Positioning**:
  - Evita Molten Metal pool sempre
  - Dodge Lava Cannon beam (perpendicular movement)
  - Exhaust Vents → reposition veloce

- **Burn strategy**:
  - Lust pull, maximize DPS
  - Heated Metallurgy stack = timer enrage soft
  - Finish fight prima stack troppo alto (healer oom)

## Strategia chiave

1. **Phase flow**:
   - Pull → split tank duo boss
   - Molten Metal → evita pool
   - Scrap Song → interrupt priority
   - Lava Cannon → dodge beam
   - Heated Metallurgy → burn prima stack alto
   - Exhaust Vents → reposition

2. **Priority list**:
   - Interrupt Scrap Song (priority #1)
   - Evitare Molten Metal pools
   - Dodge Lava Cannon beam
   - Interrupt Blazing Crescendo (priority #2)
   - Burn boss prima Heated Metallurgy overwhelm healer

3. **Common mistakes**:
   - Miss Scrap Song interrupt → shield massive DPS loss
   - Stand in Molten Metal pool → death
   - Fail dodge Lava Cannon → alto damage spike
   - Kill uno boss solo → survivor enrage wipe
   - Ignore Heated Metallurgy timer → healer oom wipe
